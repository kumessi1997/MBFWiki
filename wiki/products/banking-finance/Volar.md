## Overview

**Volar** (Latin: "palm of hand") is FPT IS's **Core Banking & Finance suite** — the back-office platform covering core banking operations, payment processing, treasury management, financial core systems, e-wallet infrastructure, and brokerage management. It is part of FPT IS's three-suite Banking & Finance portfolio alongside [[Votum]] and [[Velox]].

The suite bundles 6 standalone modules deployable independently or together, targeting commercial banks, development banks, financial corporations, and securities firms across Southeast Asia and Taiwan.

## Modules

### COBA — Core Banking System
Full-lifecycle core banking: account management, customer hierarchy, product catalog, transactions, GL, reporting. Targets banks needing to replace legacy core or launch greenfield.

**Reference clients:** CB Bank (Myanmar), ECPay (Philippines), BCG, Sacombank-SBL

---

### IPC — Intelligent Payment Center
Payment hub managing domestic and international transactions: SWIFT, ACH, real-time payment rails, FX processing, payment reconciliation, and settlement. Deep integration with core banking and external payment networks.

**Reference clients:** SCB, DBS, Standard Chartered, E.SUN Bank, OCBC, CTBC, Viet Capital Bank, Shanghai Commercial & Savings Bank

---

### WALLET — E-Wallet Core Software
Backend infrastructure for e-wallet services: wallet account management, top-up/withdrawal, peer-to-peer transfer, merchant payment, loyalty point integration, transaction limits, and compliance controls.

**Reference clients:** ECPay (Philippines)

---

### TRE — Treasury Management System
ALM and treasury operations: cash position management, liquidity forecasting, FX trading, money market instruments, interbank lending, limit management, and P&L reporting.

**Reference clients:** CB Bank (Myanmar), State Bank of Vietnam

---

### FINEX — Financial Core System
Core financial system for non-bank financial institutions: loan portfolio management, fund accounting, financial reporting, regulatory compliance, and multi-currency GL.

**Reference clients:** CB Bank (Myanmar), Sacombank-SBL

---

### IBROKER — Intelligent Brokerage Management Platform
End-to-end securities brokerage platform targeting banks' in-house brokerage arms. Tagline: "Reinvent Brokerage Services."

**Capabilities:**
- Automated sales and customer management
- Portfolio management and performance tracking
- Copy trading (follow top-performing portfolios)
- AI-powered investment community features
- Comprehensive financial reporting
- Multi-asset support

**Reference clients:** (none listed in available materials)

> [!NOTE]
> IBROKER and [[akaFortune]] appear to offer nearly identical features. IBROKER is positioned for bank brokerage departments; akaFortune is positioned for standalone securities firms. They may share a common technology base.
