## Overview

**Votum** (Latin: "vow/promise") is FPT IS's **Risk Management & Compliance suite** — the analytical and regulatory layer covering credit scoring, anti-financial crime, capital adequacy, IFRS 9 provisioning, and State Bank of Vietnam regulatory reporting. It is part of FPT IS's three-suite Banking & Finance portfolio alongside [[Volar]] and [[Velox]].

The suite bundles 5 standalone modules deployable independently. Notably, the SBRS module has the largest named client list in the entire Bank Suite — 15 banks including HSBC, JPMorgan Chase, DBS, ANZ, and Standard Chartered — demonstrating deep penetration with international banks operating in Vietnam.

## Modules

### XSCOR — Credit Scoring System
AI/ML-driven credit scoring and risk assessment: application scoring, behavioral scoring, bureau integration, scorecard management, decision engine, and reporting.

**Reference clients:** Standard Chartered, HDBank, Eximbank

---

### AFC — Anti-Financial Crime
Transaction monitoring and fraud detection: rule-based + ML anomaly detection, AML/CFT screening, sanctions list checking, suspicious activity reporting (SAR), and case management.

**Reference clients:** MobiFone Money

---

### CARS — Capital Adequacy Ratio System
Basel II/III capital adequacy computation: RWA calculation, Pillar 1/2/3 reporting, credit/market/operational risk integration, stress testing, and SBV submission.

**Reference clients:** BAC A BANK, Eximbank

---

### SBRS — State Bank Regulatory Reporting System
Automated regulatory report generation and submission to the State Bank of Vietnam (SBV). Manages the full submission lifecycle: data collection → validation → formatting → submission → acknowledgment tracking.

**Reference clients:** HSBC · Standard Chartered · JPMorgan Chase · DBS · ANZ · SMBC · BPCE · CTBC · E.SUN Bank · Bank of China · JACCS · Hong Leong Bank · Eximbank · BanViet · DongA Bank

> [!NOTE]
> SBRS has 15 named international bank clients — the largest client roster of any single module in the Bank Suite. This suggests SBRS may be the strongest entry-point product for foreign banks operating in Vietnam who need SBV compliance without replacing their global core systems.

---

### IFRS9 — IFRS 9 Expected Credit Loss System
IFRS 9 compliant expected credit loss (ECL) computation: Stage 1/2/3 classification, PD/LGD/EAD parameter estimation, ECL calculation, disclosure reporting, and audit trail.

**Reference clients:** Vietcombank, Sacombank

---

## Deployment model

- Modular — each module deployable independently without requiring other Votum or Volar modules
- Designed for integration into existing bank infrastructure (does not require FPT core banking)
- API-first architecture enabling data feeds from any core banking system

## OFSAA partnership

FPT IS is a **technical consultant for Oracle Financial Services Analytical Applications (OFSAA)** covering:
- ALM/FTP (Asset Liability Management / Funds Transfer Pricing)
- Credit Risk
- Performance Management
- Operational Risk & Compliance
- Financial Crime & Compliance

This positions Votum alongside Oracle OFSAA as complementary or alternative risk analytics — Votum is the in-house FPT IS solution; OFSAA is the Oracle enterprise option for which FPT IS provides implementation services.
