## Overview

**akaFortune** is an all-in-one financial advisory and wealth management platform developed and published by [[FPT IS]]. It is positioned as "Vietnam's Pioneering All-In-One Financial Advisory Platform." Target buyers are financial advisors, brokerages, banks, and wealth management firms that need to manage, advise on, and automate investment portfolios across multiple product types (stocks, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, margin).

akaFortune sits within [[FPT IS]]'s Banking & Finance vertical alongside the Volar (core banking), Votum (risk/compliance), and Velox (business apps) families — but targets a distinct use case: the front-office investment advisory workflow rather than back-office banking operations.

## Platform architecture

The platform is organized into three layers:

### Modules

| Module | Description |
|---|---|
| **Investment Advisory** | Structured advisory workflow for advisors managing client portfolios |
| **Investment Information Package** | Curated investment research and product data delivery |
| **Copy Trading** | Enables investors to replicate strategies of expert advisors |
| **Investment Trust** | Trust product management and administration |
| **Marketplace** | Connects investors directly with financial experts/advisors |
| **Digital Investment Community Platform** | Real-time investment discussions; professional community building |
| **Client Management** | Full client lifecycle and relationship management |
| **Sales Management** | Sales pipeline, performance tracking, channel management |

### Engines

| Engine | Description |
|---|---|
| **Investment Advisory Engine** | Automates advisory process end-to-end |
| **Performance Index Engine** | Real-time portfolio performance metrics and transaction tracking |
| **Copy Trading Engine** | Infrastructure for strategy replication |
| **Incentive Engine** | Advisor incentive and commission management |
| **Smart Placing Order** | Automated, optimized order execution |
| **Rating Engine** | Advisor and portfolio rating |
| **Portfolio Optimization** | Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT)-based optimization |
| **Backtesting** | Historical data simulation for strategy validation |

### Data layer

Client data · Investment Portfolio · Price History · Market Data

## Key capabilities

- **Portfolio construction**: MPT-based optimization; backtesting with historical data; ongoing rebalancing
- **Risk analytics**: Value-at-Risk (VaR) model; Max Drawdown; Sharpe Ratio; price-fluctuation analysis
- **Copy trading**: allows retail investors to mirror expert advisor strategies — a retail brokerage differentiator
- **Investment community**: platform-native social layer connecting investors and advisors in real time
- **Sales automation**: digitizes the full advisory sales cycle and performance management
- **Hybrid deployment**: on-premises or cloud; scalable cost model

## Competitive positioning

Three stated competitive advantages:
1. **Tailor-made for financial investment** — portfolio-centric, comprehensive product coverage, process automation
2. **Maximize operational efficiency** — digitize sales, accelerate channel growth, diversify revenue streams
3. **Deliver excellence at competitive cost** — hybrid architecture, robust security, fast deployment
