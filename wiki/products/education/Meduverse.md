## Overview

**Meduverse** is [[FPT IS]]'s game-based educational solution for primary school children (Grades 1–6), developed in association with **Oxford University Press**. It delivers Oxford curriculum content through interactive games, AI-personalized learning paths, and competitive e-sport tournaments on a web + mobile platform.

Tagline: *"Game-Based Educational Solution"*

Website: meduverse.ai

## Key differentiator

The Oxford University Press partnership is Meduverse's primary competitive advantage — no other FPT education product carries a branded international publisher content deal. English content uses the "Family and Friends" series (Grades 1–6); Math and Life Skills modules are in development.

## Value proposition

**For teachers:** Game-based lessons enhance classroom attractiveness; automated evaluation saves time; class monitoring dashboard

**For students:** Lessons become engaging games; learn at own level/pace/skill set; reward & goal-setting mechanics boost motivation

**For parents:** Monitor progress via reports; participate in child's learning; safety filtering blocks harmful content

## Content & learning system

- **English:** Oxford "Family and Friends" — Grades 1–6 (live)
- **Math (Toán):** Coming soon
- **Life Skills (Kỹ năng sống):** Coming soon
- Exercise types: Vocabulary, Grammar, Songs, Pronunciation, Games (Memory Card, Jumpy, etc.)
- AI engine personalizes content to individual student level and skill set
- Lessons and mini-games are curriculum-aligned for revision and passive knowledge reinforcement

## Competition system

- **Meduverse E-Sport** — Nationwide tournament across schools
- **[School] E-Sport** — Customized competitions within each school
- **Medu League** — Global individual tournament

## Survey results (FPT Schools, early 2025)

- **95%** of students (n=330) like playing Meduverse
- **91%** of teachers (n=35) prefer using it in teaching; **100%** say content fits
- **78%** of parents (n=273) want children to use it; only **27%** willing to purchase membership immediately

> [!NOTE]
> Parent purchase intent at 27% is relatively low — 60% need further investigation before committing. The product is still in pilot stage at FPT Schools; wider commercial rollout and pricing model not yet established.
