## Overview

**ArchiveNex** is FPT IS's comprehensive electronic archiving solution, tagline: "Preserving 'Living' Data." It is designed for organizations managing long-term and permanent records, ensuring compliance with Vietnam's revised Archiving Law. Built on the **OAIS ISO 14721:2012** international standard (Open Archival Information System), it covers the full archiving lifecycle from document submission through permanent preservation and public presentation/exhibition.

ArchiveNex is deployed across state agencies, cultural heritage organizations, education institutions, and natural resources/construction sectors. Its flagship deployment is a nationwide system for Vietnam's **Ministry of Home Affairs** (Cục Văn Thư và Lưu Trữ Nhà Nước — State Records and Archives Department).

Contact: ArchiveNex@fpt.com

## Architecture

ArchiveNex follows the OAIS 3-package model:

| Layer | Package | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Ingest | **S.I.P** (Submission Information Package) | Document collection, digitization, and submission |
| Preservation | **A.I.P** (Archival Information Package) | Long-term storage and preservation management |
| Access | **D.I.P** (Dissemination Information Package) | Retrieval, publishing, and exhibition |
