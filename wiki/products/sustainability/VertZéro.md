## Overview

**VertZéro** is [[FPT IS]]'s carbon accounting and net-zero management software platform, built to ISO 14064-1:2011 standard. It enables enterprises to measure, reduce, report, and automate their greenhouse gas (GHG) inventory across Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions — from direct operations through the full value chain.

The product is FPT IS's entry into **Sustainability Management**, a category driven by accelerating regulatory pressure: the EU's Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM, effective 2026), Vietnam's Decree 06/2022/ND-CP mandating GHG inventories for large emitters, and Vietnam's Power Plan VIII transitioning away from coal. Primary target customers are Vietnamese manufacturers exporting to Europe and large corporates with ESG disclosure obligations.

Website: https://vertzero.eco/en/

## Core Workflow

VertZéro is structured around four pillars:

1. **Measure** — Build a complete GHG inventory (Scope 1/2/3) from primary and secondary data sources
2. **Automate** — Pull data automatically from ERP/SCM systems, IoT sensors, and supplier APIs; apply emission factors from a library of 60,000+ factors
3. **Report** — Generate compliance-ready reports for CBAM, CSRD, ISSB, GHG Protocol, IPCC, SBTi, CDP, GRI, SASB
4. **Decarbonize** — Model emission reduction pathways using the Marginal Abatement Cost Curve (MACC) tool and 120+ project templates

## Scope Coverage

| Scope | Definition | Examples |
|-------|------------|---------|
| **Scope 1** | Direct GHG emissions from owned/controlled sources | Fuel combustion, industrial processes, on-site vehicles |
| **Scope 2** | Indirect emissions from purchased energy | Purchased electricity, steam, heat, cooling |
| **Scope 3** | All other indirect value-chain emissions (15 categories) | Supply chain, employee commuting, product use & end-of-life, business travel, logistics |

## Key Features

### Emission Factor Database
- 60,000+ emission factors sourced from IPCC, US EPA, DEFRA, Ecoinvent, and custom enterprise libraries
- Supports location-based and market-based Scope 2 accounting methods

### Decarbonization Planning
- **Marginal Abatement Cost Curve (MACC)** — visualizes cost vs. impact of each reduction initiative; prioritizes actions by cost-effectiveness
- **120+ project templates** — covers energy efficiency, renewable energy, process electrification, logistics optimization, and more
- **SBTi compliance checker** — validates whether proposed targets align with Science Based Targets initiative thresholds

### Supply Chain & Vendor Management
- **Vendor Emissions Management** — tracks Tier 1 and Tier 2 supplier emissions; sends data collection requests; consolidates responses
- **Procurement Emission Management** — embeds carbon cost into procurement decisions
- **Automated Data Integration** — API connections to supplier ERP/SCM systems for automated activity data pull

### Employee Engagement
- **Employee Engagement Platform** — mobile app for employees to log commuting patterns and travel; feeds Scope 3 Category 7 data automatically

### Compliance Reporting
- Native report templates for: **CBAM** (EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism), **CSRD** (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive), **ISSB** (International Sustainability Standards Board), **CDP**, **GRI**, **SASB**, **SBTi**, **GHG Protocol**

## Market Context

- Vietnam ranks **3rd in Southeast Asia** for GHG emissions
- **CBAM Phase 2 (from 2026)**: EU imposes carbon tariffs on cement, steel, aluminum, fertilizers, electricity, hydrogen — sectors where Vietnamese exporters face direct exposure
- **Decree 06/2022/ND-CP**: Vietnamese government mandates GHG inventories for enterprises above emissions thresholds
- **Power Plan VIII**: Vietnam's energy transition plan phasing out coal power, creating pressure on energy-intensive industries
- **Vietnam net-zero target**: 2050, aligned with Paris Agreement commitments

---

## Target Customers

- Vietnamese manufacturers exporting to the EU (CBAM-exposed sectors: steel, cement, aluminum, fertilizers)
- Large Vietnamese corporates with international investors or stock exchange ESG disclosure requirements
- Enterprises under Decree 06/2022/ND-CP mandatory GHG reporting scope
- Companies seeking SBTi validation or CDP submission
