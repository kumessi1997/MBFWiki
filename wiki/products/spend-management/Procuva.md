# Procuva

**Procuva** is FPT IS's **Procurement & Bidding Management System**, designed to digitize and automate the full **Procure-to-Pay (P2P)** lifecycle for enterprises. Tagline: *"Empower Smart Procurement."*

## Overview

Procuva targets enterprises running manual procurement operations — unable to control costs, prone to data-entry errors, and lacking supplier process visibility. It provides a centralized, standardized, digitally-signed procurement process across two integrated portals: an Internal Portal for procurement teams and a Supplier Portal for vendors. The product philosophy is **Ceaseless Improvement**: a 6-step cycle (Identify Requirements → Analyze Data → Define Scope → Develop Plan → Implement Plan → Evaluate Results) deployed alongside the customer on an ongoing basis.

## Core Modules

| Module | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Budget Management** | Real-time budget allocation and transaction monitoring; continuous budget vs. actual checking |
| **Procurement Management** | Purchase requests → supplier selection → ordering → receiving |
| **Payment Management** | Automates payment processing; links to contracts, orders, and invoices for accuracy |
| **Supplier Portal** | Supplier registration, profile, 360° feedback, invoice publishing, payment requests, multi-round bidding |

## System Architecture

**Internal Portal:**
- *Supplier Management:* Onboarding, Qualification, Life Cycle management, Performance tracking, Risk Management
- *Catalogue Management:* Item Sorting, Cart & Wishlist, Catalogue Management
- *Procurement Operations (full cycle):* Budget Control → Procurement Planning → Requesting → Sourcing → Negotiation → Selection → Contract Management → Ordering & Receiving → Payment → Reports & Analytics

**Supplier Portal:**
- *Supplier Management:* Registration, Profile Management, 360° Feedback, Delivery Management, Invoice Publishing, Payment Request
- *Catalogue Management:* Catalogue Creation, Interface, Management
- *Quotation & Bidding:* RFI/RFQ Documents, RFI/RFQ Response, Document Storage
