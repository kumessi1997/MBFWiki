## Overview

**FPT Information System Company Limited (FPT IS)** is a wholly owned subsidiary of [[FPT Corporation]], established December 31, 1994. Headquartered at 10 Pham Van Bach Street, Cau Giay Ward, Hanoi. It is the product and enterprise software arm of FPT — distinct from FPT Software (which does outsourcing) and FPT Smart Cloud (infrastructure).

**Mission:** Constantly innovating to make transactions between governments, businesses, and citizens fast, efficient, and reliable.

## Scale

- 3,700+ employees; 2,400+ senior consultants and internationally certified engineers
- 2,400+ international certificates (Cisco, IBM, Microsoft, Oracle, SAP, HP, etc.)
- 2,900+ clients; 150+ in Vietnam's VNR500; 100+ global clients
- 200+ awards over 30 years; 120+ for "Made by FPT" products; 30+ international awards

## Quality certifications

- CMMI Dev 3.0 — Maturity Level 5 (highest software process maturity rating)
- ISO 27001 (information security)
- ISO 20000 (IT service management)
