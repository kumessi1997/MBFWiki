## Central Retail Vietnam (CRV)

Vietnamese subsidiary of Central Retail Corporation (Thai retail conglomerate). One of akaBot's flagship retail reference customers.

Sources: [[2025-06-01_akabot-ecosystem-brochure]], [[2025-06-01_akabot-industry-brochures]]

## akaBot automation

**Problem:** 1.8M invoices/year, 180,000 working hours/year consumed, high staff turnover due to repetitive work.

**Solution:** Invoice matching automation in Finance & Accounting (1 process to start).

**Results:**
- 1.8M invoices/year processed automatically (75% of total)
- 90,000 working hours saved per year
- $500,000 operational costs saved over 5 years
- Reduced staff turnover
