## Overview

**JACCS** (Japan Consumer Credit Co., Ltd.) is a major Japanese consumer finance company. Its Vietnam arm is **JIVF (JACCS International Vietnam Finance Company Limited)** — one of 16 licensed consumer finance companies (CTTCs) in Vietnam.

JIVF is the flagship customer and primary case study for [[LendVero]], [[FPT IS]]'s lending platform.

## LendVero case study results

- **70% reduction** in loan processing time (from ~40–45 min to significantly less)
- **100% eKYC accuracy** using OCR ID card + NFC + Liveness + Ministry of Public Security verification
- Deployed both LOS (Loan Origination System) and (implied) Debt Collection modules

## Context

JACCS/JIVF represents the "Japan financial brand expanding into Vietnam consumer finance" archetype — similar to other Japanese finance companies (AEON Finance, SHB Finance) that have entered Vietnam. Their adoption of LendVero signals that FPT IS can win enterprise-grade deals with foreign-invested CTTCs.

> [!NOTE]
> The brochure and saleskit name JACCS as the sole named case study. The saleskit mentions "20+ Digital Onboarding clients" and "19 IDcheck clients" as aggregate references but no other named CTTC customers are disclosed.
