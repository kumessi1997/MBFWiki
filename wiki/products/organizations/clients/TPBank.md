## TPBank

Vietnamese commercial bank; one of the country's top digital transformation leaders in banking. [[akaBot]]'s flagship Vietnamese reference customer.

Sources: [[2025-06-01_akabot-ecosystem-brochure]], [[2025-06-01_akabot-industry-brochures]]

## akaBot automation journey

| Date | Stage | Scale |
|---|---|---|
| Sep 2019 | PoC | 3 processes, 2 BUs |
| Nov 2019 | Pilot | 14 processes, 5 BUs |
| Jul 2020 | Mass rollout | 60+ processes, 10 BUs |
| Dec 2022 | Full implementation | 400+ bots, 16 BUs |
| 2024 | End-to-end intelligent automation | 500+ processes (incl. 70+ IA), 17 BUs, 20 end-to-end processes |

## Results

- 310,000 working hours saved per year
- 98% human error reduction
- 500+ automated tasks across 17 business units
