## FPT Corporation

Vietnam's largest IT company. Operates across software, IT services, telecommunications, and education. The MBF product catalog (source: [[2025-04-10_fpt-mbf-product-catalog]]) covers the B2B software portfolio of the FPT IS (Information Systems) division.

## Product portfolio structure

Two classification axes:
1. **By functional category** — what the product does (8 categories: business process transformation, financial management, digital operations, HR, service management, sustainability, CRM, supply chain)
2. **By vertical industry** — which sector it serves (healthcare, education, manufacturing, hospitality, government)

## Key product families

- **[[akaBot]]** — RPA/hyperautomation; flagship export product, 21 countries
- **akaMES** — Manufacturing execution system; multiple industry variants
- **UTOP** — Loyalty/CRM platform suite
- **FPT.eInvoice / FPT.TVAN** — Electronic invoicing; authorized by Vietnam Tax Authority
- **FPT.EagleEye** — Cybersecurity monitoring (SOC, malware, vulnerability assessment)
- **FPT.eHospital / FPT.EMR** — Hospital management and electronic medical records
- **FPT.LGSP / FPT.dGOV** — e-Government platform suite for local governments
- **[[FPT Semiconductor]]** — Chip design; semiconductor design pioneer in Vietnam

## Market position

- Largest IT company in Vietnam
- akaBot operates in 21 countries with 3,900+ customers
- FPT.eInvoice described as one of the first Vietnamese e-invoice solutions authorized by the Tax Authority
