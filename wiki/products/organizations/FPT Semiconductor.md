## FPT Semiconductor (Fsemi)

Chip design subsidiary of [[FPT Corporation]]. One of Vietnam's pioneer companies in semiconductor design. Specializes in the **design phase** of the chip supply chain (fabless model — design only, no fabrication). (source: [[2025-04-10_fpt-mbf-product-catalog]])

## Significance

Vietnam has been actively developing a semiconductor industry as part of its broader technology ambitions. FPT Semiconductor represents one of the few Vietnamese-owned IC design efforts.
