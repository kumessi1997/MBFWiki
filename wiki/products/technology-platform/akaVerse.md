## Overview

**akaVerse** is FPT IS's Virtual Reality / Augmented Reality / Extended Reality (VR/AR/XR) and immersive technology subsidiary, founded in September 2021. It develops and delivers enterprise-grade immersive experiences — virtual tours, virtual shops, virtual training, digital twins, metaverse environments, and volumetric content — primarily for Vietnamese and Asian enterprise clients. It operates as a distinct unit within [[FPT IS]]'s product portfolio.

## Business unit structure

akaVerse serves two distinct customer segments within [[FPT IS]]:
- **Government Sector (GS)**: 3D design, VR, AR, 360/drone content for government and public institutions
- **Enterprise Sector (ES)**: Virtual Tour, 3D digitization, virtual training for corporate clients

## Competitive positioning

### Virtual Tour vs. Matterport

| Feature | akaVerse | Matterport |
|---|---|---|
| Input | 3D design + real photos | Physical scan only |
| Movement | Freely renderable | Limited to scan points |
| Post-processing | Supported | Not supported |
| Hosting | On-prem or akaVerse | Matterport only |
| Domain | Custom | Matterport domain |
| Analytics | Preset metrics | Fully customizable |

akaVerse wins on deployment flexibility, customizability, and data ownership. Matterport wins on analytics customization.
