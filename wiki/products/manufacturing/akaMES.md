## Overview

**akaMES** is a Digital Manufacturing Platform and Intelligent MOM (Manufacturing Operations Management) solution developed by [[FPT IS]] (website: akames.io). Founded in 2019, it controls shopfloor production, enables cross-department collaboration, and extends ERP systems with manufacturing-specific capabilities across the full production value chain. It is positioned as "the first product implementing AI in real-time production operations in the world" and costs "only 1/3 of international solutions."

akaMES is sold and deployed by [[FPT IS]] under the Manufacturing vertical. Its product family (akaMES + akaPLM + STMA + TOMI + Ext-WH + DW) forms a comprehensive manufacturing digital transformation ecosystem.

## Platform architecture

### Four core solution modules

| Module | Description |
|---|---|
| **EMS** (Energy Management) | Monitor electricity/water/gas consumption per factory; compare across multi-factory Head Office view |
| **OEE & MA** (Overall Equipment Effectiveness & Maintenance) | Machine issue early warning via cameras/sensors; root cause analysis; predictive maintenance; OEE per workstation |
| **QMS** (Quality Management) | Factory-wide quality monitoring; SPC charts; sampling management; abnormal case summary; Head Office sync |
| **MES** (Manufacturing Execution) | AI-powered production scheduling; work order management; full bi-directional traceability (raw → finished); integrates with SAP, Dynamics, Oracle |

### Core module features (8 manufacturing eco-system areas)

Advanced Scheduling · Production · Warehouse · Facility · Quality · Insights · Integrity · Paperless

### Generative AI capabilities

- **Advanced Scheduling**: AI-driven schedule optimization from historical data; resource analysis automation
- **Dashboard with Chatbot**: Natural language queries on production data for tailored recommendations
- **Early Warning**: Predictive maintenance scheduling based on operational reports
- **Anomaly Detection**: Deep Learning-based root cause identification; reduces investigation time
