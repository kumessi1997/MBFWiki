## Overview

**Azladin** is FPT IS's **Digital Healthcare Platform** — a cloud SaaS product that unifies hospital and clinical systems across Vietnam through standards-based interoperability. It provides the foundational infrastructure layer for health information exchange: connecting patient records, medical imaging, clinical decision support, lab systems, and administrative workflows across individual hospitals and multi-facility networks.

Tagline: "Connected services · Better insights · Better care"

Azladin is distinct from [[AZINSU]] (insurance claims). Together they form FPT IS's full healthcare vertical: **Azladin = clinical/hospital layer; AZINSU = insurance/claims layer**.

Contact: contact@fpt.com · fpt-is.com

## Target users

Patients · Doctors · Partners · Hospital Staff

## Platform architecture — 6 functional domains

| Domain | Key components |
|---|---|
| **Clinical Data Sharing** | Patient demographics, encounters, allergies, history, lab results, radiology reports/images, medications, immunizations, diagnoses, ECG, spirometry, vital signs, clinical documents, appointments |
| **Registries & eMPI Service** | Master Patient Index; Organization Registry; Practitioner Registry |
| **Terminology** | Snomed-CT · LOINC · ICD10 · HL7 FHIR |
| **Services** | SMART-on-FHIR; SMART Health Link; International Patient Summary; Clinical Decision Support; Shared Health Record; Data Analytics; Clinical Evidence Analysis (evidence-based medicine) |
| **Integration** | HIS/EMR, LIS, RIS/PACs; Mobile apps (doctors/nurses); KIOSK; Website; Appointments; Social Networks; CRM |
| **Repositories** | Clinical Data Repository; Document Data Repository; Imaging Data Repository; Data Analytics Repository; Data Research Repository |

## International standards compliance

- **HL7 FHIR** — International health data exchange standard
- **SMART-on-FHIR** — App authorization framework for clinical apps
- **SMART Health Link** — Patient-held health credentials
- **International Patient Summary** — WHO/IHE standard for patient portability
- **Snomed-CT** — International clinical terminology
- **LOINC** — Lab and clinical observation codes
- **ICD10** — International disease classification
