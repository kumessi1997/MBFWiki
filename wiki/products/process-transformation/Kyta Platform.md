## Overview

**Kyta Platform** is [[FPT IS]]'s all-in-one digital identification and agreement management platform. Tagline: *"Pioneering the Digital Transformation of Identification and Agreement."* Evolved from FPT.eContract (standalone e-contract product) through several phases into a full iPaaS (Integration Platform as a Service) covering the entire identification-to-agreement lifecycle.

**Website:** kyta.fpt.com | **Support:** 1900.636.191 ext 3 | customersupport@fpt.com

**Scale:** 4,800+ enterprises and organizations (as of 2026)

**Origin:** FPT.eContract → document standardization/DMS → Low-code/No-code + AI → Kyta Platform (connect, integrate, automate)

## Products

### Kyta ALM — Agreement Lifecycle Management

Comprehensive digital agreement management from creation through post-completion.

**Key features:**
- Identity verification for individuals and businesses
- Digitized workflows with robust internal/external system integration
- Unified platform connecting businesses, governments, and individuals
- Real-time tracking with transparency for all agreements
- No-code/low-code — no programming required

### Kyta Signature — E-Signature

Secure, certified electronic contract signing.

**Key features:**
- Remote digital signatures with TSA (timestamping) + LTV (long-term validation)
- Certified by Ministry of Industry and Trade (legal validity)
- Advanced encryption; tamper-proof
- PIPA-compliant eKYC for Korean market (international expansion)

### Kyta Gate — Identity & Agreement Platform

End-to-end identification and agreement for high-compliance use cases (banking, finance, insurance, government).

**12 submodules:**
- eAccount, eTask, eForm, eFlow, eRequest, eWriter
- FPT.eContract (e-signing), FPT.CeCA (document authentication)
- eConDrive (storage), eCatalogue (catalog mgmt), eID (identity), eLMS (loan management)

**Primary verticals:** Banking onboarding, consumer finance (installment loans), insurance contracts, securities onboarding, government admin

## Technology & Security

- **No-code/low-code:** drag-and-drop workflow and form builder
- **eKYC:** NFC ID card, IDCheck, Liveness, Ministry of Public Security (MPS) cross-check — claims 100% accuracy
- **E-signature legal stack:** TSA + LTV + FPT.CeCA (Ministry of Industry & Trade certified)
- **Certifications:** ISO 9001:2008, ISO 27001, CMMI3, PCI DSS (FPT Smart Cloud), eKYC FaceID Biometric (ISO 30107 Level 1 & 2)
- **Multi-layer security:** Firewall, WAF, Data Encryption, Multi-Tenancy, Pentest
- **Deployment:** FPT Cloud (primary); AWS/Google Cloud compatible; private cloud option; 24/7 support
