## Agentic Process Automation (APA)

The next evolution beyond [[RPA and Hyperautomation]]. Where RPA handles rule-based, deterministic tasks ("do"), APA handles complex, context-dependent tasks that require judgment ("think, learn, do, make decisions"). Positioned as handling the ~20% of enterprise tasks that RPA cannot automate.

## RPA vs. APA

| Dimension | RPA | APA |
|---|---|---|
| Task type | Rule-based, structured | Complex, needs intelligence |
| Approach | "Do" | "Think, learn, do, decide" |
| Bot management | Manual control | Intelligent, autonomous |
| Data | Structured inputs | Unstructured or ambiguous inputs |
