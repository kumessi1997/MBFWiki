## Intelligent Document Processing (IDP)

AI-powered extraction and processing of data from unstructured documents — PDFs, scanned images, handwritten forms, invoices, contracts. Combines OCR (optical character recognition) with ML/NLP to understand document structure and extract meaning, not just text.

IDP is significant because ~80% of valuable enterprise data exists in unstructured form (cited by akaBot). Traditional RPA can only handle structured, predictable inputs — IDP extends automation reach into document-heavy workflows.

## Role in akaBot ecosystem

Implemented as **akaBot Vision**. Plugs into RPA workflows to process documents before structured bot actions. Used heavily in:
- Invoice matching/processing (retail, finance)
- Loan disbursement validation (banking)
- Health records processing (insurance/healthcare)
- Mortgage pre-approval (banking)

## Example impact

- Central Retail Vietnam: 1.8M invoices/year processed automatically
- Insurance health records: 50% cases/day automated, 75% time reduction, 4,200 hrs/year previously required by 2 employees
