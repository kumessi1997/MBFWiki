## Center of Excellence (RPA)

An internal organizational unit that governs, scales, and continuously improves a company's RPA/automation program. Abbreviated CoE. A standard milestone in [[RPA and Hyperautomation]] maturity — without it, automation programs tend to plateau at a small number of processes.

## Why it matters

RPA deployments commonly start with 1–5 pilot processes and fail to scale because:
- No central ownership of bot governance
- Inconsistent development standards
- No process discovery discipline
- Change management neglected

A CoE solves these by centralizing expertise, standards, and pipeline management.

## akaBot's CoE model (per [[2025-06-01_akabot-ecosystem-brochure]])

akaBot calls its methodology "Fast to Mass":
1. **Assessment & PoC** (2 wks) — discover and prioritize 1–5 processes
2. **Implementation** (5+ wks) — build, test, go live
3. **Maintenance & Support** — 24/7 operation
4. **Mass Automation + CoE** — scale to 500+ processes with internal ownership

TPBank is the best example: started with 3 processes in 2 BUs (2019) → 500+ processes in 17 BUs with an active CoE and 70+ intelligent automation processes (2024).
