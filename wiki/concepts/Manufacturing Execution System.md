## Definition

A **Manufacturing Execution System (MES)** is enterprise software that manages, monitors, and controls manufacturing operations on the shopfloor in real time. It sits between business-level ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems and plant-level control systems (PLC/SCADA/HMI), connecting planning and execution. Modern MES — often called **MOM (Manufacturing Operations Management)** — extends beyond shopfloor control to include energy management, predictive maintenance, quality management, and AI-driven scheduling.

The global MES market was approximately $15B in 2022 and is projected to reach $40B by 2030 at a CAGR of 13.1%.

## Core capabilities

- **Production management**: scheduling, work order management, operator execution, progress tracking
- **Quality management (QMS)**: inspection plans, SPC charts, traceability, non-conformance management
- **Asset/facility management**: maintenance scheduling, OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) tracking, predictive maintenance
- **Traceability**: full bi-directional tracking of raw materials → semi-finished → finished goods
- **Warehouse management**: receipt/issue of materials, on-hand balance, WIP tracking
- **Integration**: connects upward to ERP (SAP, Dynamics, Oracle) and downward to PLC/SCADA machines
- **KPIs & analytics**: real-time dashboards, OEE, production efficiency, energy consumption

## MOM vs. MES distinction

**MES** historically focused narrowly on shopfloor execution. **MOM** (Manufacturing Operations Management) is the broader term adopted by platforms like akaMES that include EMS (energy), OEE/maintenance, QMS, and advanced AI scheduling alongside traditional MES functions. The industry is converging on MOM as the standard term for full-stack digital manufacturing platforms.

## Vertical specialization

MES platforms are increasingly sold as industry-specific vertical solutions rather than generic platforms. Examples from [[akaMES]]:
- **eBR** (Electronic Batch Record) for pharma/healthcare
- **PM** for pharmaceutical production
- **HDM** for medical device manufacturing (FDA/GAMP5 compliant)
- **FC** for fluid/chemical/FMCG
- **EDS** for electronics assembly
- **MSA** for steel/aluminum

[[akaMES]] (by [[FPT IS]]) is Vietnam's leading domestically built MES/MOM platform, with 30+ clients across 10+ countries. It competes directly against global players Siemens Opcenter, DELMIA Apriso, and AVEVA MES, positioning on cost (1/3 of international pricing), customizability, and local market expertise. IBM and Yokogawa have both endorsed akaMES for international use.

Target buyers in Vietnam: FDI enterprises (Japanese, Korean, European manufacturers with factories in Vietnam) and Vietnamese manufacturers in heavy industry, automotive, steel, pharma, and FMCG.
