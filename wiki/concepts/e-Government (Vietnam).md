## e-Government (Vietnam)

Vietnam's program to digitize government services at national and provincial levels. [[FPT Corporation]] is one of the dominant platform providers, particularly at the provincial/local government level.

## FPT government product suite (per [[2025-04-10_fpt-mbf-product-catalog]])

| Product | Description |
|---|---|
| FPT.LGSP | Local Government Service Platform — integration layer for provincial e-government |
| FPT.LCD | Provincial shared platform; includes multiple software modules |
| FPT.dGOV | Digital government operations |
| FPT.dCitizen | Citizen-facing digital services |
| FPT.dOffice | Digital office / internal government workflow |
| FPT.eGOV | e-Government platform |
| FPT.eDistrict | District-level digital services |
| FPT.SmartKiosk | Physical kiosks for citizen service access |
| FPT.eProcurement | Government procurement digitization |
