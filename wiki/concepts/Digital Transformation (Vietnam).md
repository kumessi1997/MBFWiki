## Digital Transformation (Vietnam)

Vietnam's national and enterprise-level push to digitize government services, business processes, and key industries. [[FPT Corporation]] is a major enabler, with products spanning e-invoicing, e-government, electronic contracts, digital HR, and manufacturing digitization.

## Key government programs addressed by FPT products

- **e-Invoice mandate** — Vietnam's Tax Authority requires electronic invoicing; FPT.eInvoice and FPT.TVAN are authorized solutions
- **e-Government** — FPT.LGSP (Local Government Service Platform), FPT.dGOV, FPT.dCitizen provide the infrastructure for digital public services at the provincial level
- **National digital identity** — FPT.eKYC, FPT eID support digital identity verification tied to Vietnam's national ID system

## Enterprise digitization themes (from FPT catalog)

- Business process automation ([[akaBot]], UBot, Kyta Platform)
- Paperless operations (FPT.Spro, FPT.eContract, FPT.eSign)
- HR digitization (FPT.iHRP, FPT People X Hiring, FPT.eLearning)
- Sustainability reporting (VertZéro, Aura Network)
- Hospital digitization (FPT.eHospital, FPT.EMR)
