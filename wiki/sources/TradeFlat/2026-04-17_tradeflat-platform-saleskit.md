## Summary

TradeFlat is FPT IS's digital financial ecosystem platform that connects Banks and Enterprises through a suite of five integrated products: Trade Finance, Supply Chain Finance, Business Financial Health Analytics & Monitor, Customs Clearance, and eKYB. The platform applies digital transformation technologies (OCR, AI, eKYC, e-signature, ERP integration) to digitize the full lifecycle of corporate banking transactions — from enterprise onboarding to import/export trade finance, domestic supply chain lending, and customs declaration.

The saleskit (46 slides, July 2025) covers FPT/FPT IS credentials, the TradeFlat ecosystem overview, detailed architecture per product module, case studies (BIDV and Synnex FPT), customer testimonials from six named banks, and TradeFlat's international positioning through partnerships with TradeWaltz (Japan), TradeWindow (NZ/Australia), and E&P International (China). A key highlight is the TradeFlat–TradeWaltz integration project subsidized by Japan's Ministry of Economy, Trade & Industry (METI), with a successful PoC in January 2024.

The platform's unique value is its ability to act as a neutral hub bridging corporate ERPs, Vietnam state systems (VNACCS customs, Tax Authority), and bank core banking systems — while bundling eKYB onboarding, e-signing, and financial health monitoring in a single platform.

## Key facts

- Developed by **FPT IS** (FPT Information System Company Limited)
- 5 core products: Trade Finance, Supply Chain Finance, Business Financial Health Monitor, Customs Clearance, eKYB
- **Trade Finance modules**: L/C (domestic & international), eGuarantee (domestic & international), Collection (outgoing/incoming), Trade Loans / Import shipment financing
- **Supply Chain Finance**: Supplier Finance (AP-based), Distributor Finance (AR-based), Factoring, Reverse Factoring, Pre-shipment financing
- **Business Financial Health Monitor**: Dashboard (6 indices: Financial Index, Performance Efficiency, Cashflow Index, Liquidity, Profitability, Growth Capability), Monitor & Alert, Credit Scorecard & configuration
- **Customs Clearance**: Import & Export customs declaration portal integrated with VNACCS and Vietnam Customs Payment Portal; Green/Yellow/Red lane support
- **eKYB**: Digital identity & business verification; OCR + Facial Recognition + AI; Real-time check against government & tax databases; fraud detection
- Architecture layers: Financial Integration Hub (Corebank, ECM, e-Signature) → General Modules + TradeHub Module + Trade Finance + SCF + Supporting Services → Corp Integration Hub (Anchor ERP, Partner Trade Portal, 3rd-party trade platforms) → 3rd Party Verification (State Authority, Tax)
- **Named bank partners**: BIDV, VPBank, MSB, Techcombank, TPBank, Vietcombank, Sacombank
- **Named enterprise partners**: SYNNEX FPT (ICT distributor), Thanh Long (durable goods), Ba Huan (consumer goods)
- **International partners**: TradeWaltz (Japan), TradeWindow (Australia/NZ), E&P International (China)
- FPT IS is a member of **PAA.net (Digital Trade Alliance)** since November 2023
- **TradeFlat–TradeWaltz integration project**: subsidized by Japan METI; PoC Jan 2024; participants: Mizuho Bank, Vietcombank, Mitsubishi Corporation, Sojitz Corporation
- MOU with **TradeWindow** (NZ) signed April 2025 — cross-border Vietnam–NZ trade
- MOU exchanges at **ASEAN-Japan Co-creation Forum**; witnessed by Prime Minister of Vietnam (Dec 2023, 50th anniversary Vietnam–Japan)
