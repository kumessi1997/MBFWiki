## Summary

Three English-language vertical brochures covering akaBot's application in Banking, Manufacturing, and Retail. All follow the same structure as the main ecosystem brochure but with industry-specific use cases and pain points. Published June 2025.

The core platform pitch is identical across all three. Key differentiation is in the **priority process lists** and **industry-specific case studies**.

---

## Banking vertical

### Top automation processes
1. Card center — international transaction processing (incoming/outgoing)
2. Operations — credit card debt settlement
3. Risk management — fraud transaction detection
4. Finance — reserve requirement tracking
5. Finance — regulatory violation reporting

### Key case studies
- **TPBank** — 500+ bots across 17 BUs, 310,000 hrs/year saved, 98% human error reduced. Journey: PoC (3 processes, 2 BUs, 2019) → mass automation (500+ processes, 2024)
- **Loan disbursement validation** — 500 cases/day automated, 90% processing time reduced, 98% accuracy
- **Mortgage pre-approval** — 90% process automated, 100% accuracy, using RPA + IDP/OCR + Chatbot
- **International transaction data receipt** — 1,000+ transactions/day, SLA 100%, deployed in 2.5 weeks

## Manufacturing vertical

### Top automation processes
1. R&D — data collection & analysis
2. Supply chain — supplier relationship management
3. Production — product code/info extraction & reconciliation
4. Logistics — transportation & warehouse management
5. Finance — invoice processing

### Key case studies

- **CAD drawing processing** — 90% of processing time saved, 80% of human resources freed, 24/7 operation
- **UK manufacturer** — 4 processes in 5 months, 80% processing time reduction
- **CV processing with APA** — 70% screening time saved, 40% candidate quality increase

## Retail vertical

### Retail-specific pain points
- Omni-channel complexity
- 200,000+ invoices/month processing load
- Supply chain disruption and cost optimization
- Manual tasks prevent rapid scaling
- Insufficient 24/7 customer support

### Top automation processes
1. Finance — invoice incoming/outgoing
2. Purchasing — purchase order processing
3. Purchasing — service charge reports
4. Customer service — delivery note creation
5. HR — employee onboarding

### Key case studies
- **Central Retail Vietnam (CRV)** — 1.8M invoices/year automated (75% of total), $500K saved in 5 years, 90,000 hrs/year saved
- **Purchase request processing (logistics)** — 1,000+ items/day, SLA 100% guaranteed, processing reduced from 45 min to 5 min
- **AI chatbot for internal knowledge queries** — 95% reduction in information query time, uses Agentic Automation

---

## Cross-vertical pattern: "Fast to Mass" methodology

Consistent 4-stage rollout model:
1. **Assessment & PoC** (2 weeks) — identify 1–5 processes, internal training
2. **Implementation** (5+ weeks) — bot development, integration, go-live
3. **Maintenance & Support** — 24/7 operation, change management
4. **Mass Automation** — scale to 500+ processes, establish CoE
