## Summary

FPT IS's Banking & Finance portfolio is organized into three branded suites — **Volar** (Core Banking & Finance), **Votum** (Risk Management & Compliance), and **Velox** (Business Applications) — plus a layer of Other Platforms (OFSAA, akaBot, LendVero, eSign, EMS, akaFortune, TradeFlat) and dedicated Support Services. Together they represent 30 years of BFSI experience, 1,500+ experts across 3 development centers (Hanoi, Da Nang, HCMC), and 100+ banks and financial institutions served across Vietnam and the region.

The 46-page Total Offerings deck (Feb 2016, but product names and customer references are current) maps the full 7-layer MBF Banking Landscape: Customer Engagement → Channel Management → Business Applications → Backend Systems → Data & Analytics → Cyber Security → IT Infrastructure. Each suite maps to one or more layers in this stack.

Individual brochures for each of the 17 module products were also ingested from the Bank Suit folder.

## Key facts

- FPT IS BFSI credentials: 30 years, 100+ banks/FIs, 1,500+ BFSI experts, 3 development centers
- Three primary suites cover the full BSS stack for banking and finance
- Volar = back-office core (Core Banking, Payment, Treasury, Financial Core, E-Wallet, Brokerage)
- Votum = risk and compliance (Credit Scoring, Anti-Financial Crime, CARS, IFRS9, Regulatory Reporting)
- Velox = business applications (Digital Banking, Teller, Insurance, Cash Transport, ECM, HRM, Sales)
- Total: 17 named modules across 3 suites
- Vietnam Big 4 banks (Vietcombank, BIDV, VietinBank implied; Sacombank, Eximbank, HDBank named) are clients
- Regional presence: Myanmar (CB Bank), Philippines (ECPay), Thailand (Kasikornbank), Taiwan (E.SUN, CTBC), Singapore (DBS, OCBC, Standard Chartered, UOB)
