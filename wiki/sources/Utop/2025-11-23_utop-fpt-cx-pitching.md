## Summary

This is the FPT CX Suite pitch deck presented at **Make in Vietnam Day**, November 23, 2025. It provides the most recent (Nov 2025) articulation of FPT CX Suite's architecture, product strategy, and case study portfolio. The "Make in Vietnam" framing emphasizes FPT CX Suite as a domestically built AI product competing at international enterprise scale — consistent with the broader Vietnamese government push for locally developed software to displace foreign vendors.

The deck presents the full platform architecture across six pillars: Loyalty OS, UEngage (CDP), UReach (AI CRM Agent using LAM), UAgent (AI Agent Builder), Promotion Engine, and Commerce OS (UPOS + Headless Commerce). It explicitly calls out AI/ML capabilities including predictive churn modeling, next-best-offer recommendations, and agentic outreach campaigns.

Key case studies highlighted: TPBank, Cathay Financial Holdings, Unilever Vietnam, Maison Retail, FamilyMart, VPBank. The deck is the most current source in the Utop folder.

## Key facts

- Presented: November 23, 2025 (Make in Vietnam Day)
