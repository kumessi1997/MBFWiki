## Summary

This is a two-page saleskit document for **Utop atWork**, a B2B2E (business-to-business-to-employee) platform that extends Utop's loyalty points ecosystem into the workplace. It targets HR departments and employers seeking to replace manual, fragmented employee benefit administration with a digitized, automated platform.

Utop atWork allows employees to earn and spend **Utop points** across canteen services, shopping, entertainment, and partner rewards — all within a single app. For employers, it reduces HR admin burden by automating benefit disbursement, tracking, and reporting. The platform leverages Utop's existing consumer loyalty infrastructure, positioning atWork as an enterprise extension of the Utop ecosystem.

## Key facts

- Target buyer: HR departments at enterprises
- Employee touchpoints: canteen, shopping, entertainment, partner rewards
- Currency: Utop points (shared with consumer loyalty platform)
- Value proposition: reduces HR administrative burden; digitizes benefit management
- Document version: v1.0 (likely 2025 based on folder context)
- Positions Utop as both B2C (consumer loyalty) and B2B2E (employee benefits)
