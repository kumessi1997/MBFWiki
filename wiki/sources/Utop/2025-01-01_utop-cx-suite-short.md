## Summary

This is Utop's short sales deck for FPT CX Suite, presenting the full product architecture, competitive positioning, and enterprise case studies. It is the most detailed single document on the platform's capabilities and real-world deployments. The deck covers all six core platform modules, AI/ML capabilities, integration architecture, and key reference accounts across Vietnam and internationally (Taiwan, global).

FPT CX Suite is positioned against Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Bizfly, Getfly, Comarch, and Oracle — differentiated by faster deployment, lower cost, Southeast Asia localization, and AI-native design using a **Large Action Model (LAM)** for UReach.

Case studies in this deck include TPBank (Vietnam), Cathay Financial Holdings (Taiwan), Unilever Vietnam, Maison Retail, FamilyMart Vietnam, VPBank, HCMC Metro, and ShinhanLife.

## Key facts

- FPT CX Suite competitive positioning: faster deployment and lower TCO than Salesforce/Dynamics 365
- UReach uses LAM (Large Action Model) for AI-driven outreach — distinct from standard LLM chatbots
- TPBank: 6M loyalty members, 1M transactions/day, 200 transactions/second peak load
- Cathay Financial Holdings (Taiwan): 14.4M members, 200 transactions/second
- Unilever Vietnam: 27% revenue boost, all brands unified on one platform
- Maison Retail: 35% up-sales boost, 2M members, 4-month deployment
- FamilyMart Vietnam: 100K+ MAU, 5-year partnership
- VPBank: 5,000+ partners on platform
- Architecture: microservices, API-first, supports on-prem and SaaS
