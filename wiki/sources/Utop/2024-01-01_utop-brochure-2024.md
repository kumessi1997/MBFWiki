## Summary

This is Utop's 2024 product brochure, presenting the **FPT CX Suite** as the branded umbrella for Utop's full platform portfolio. Utop is introduced as a member company of FPT Corporation, backed by FPT and SBI Holdings, operating at utop.io. The brochure positions FPT CX Suite as an AI-embedded, end-to-end customer experience and loyalty platform targeting enterprise clients across banking, retail, telecom, and insurance verticals.

The suite is built on a microservices architecture with on-premises and SaaS deployment options. It carries ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II certifications and is available on Microsoft Azure Marketplace. The brochure emphasizes low-code customization, omni-channel reach, and fast deployment timelines (4–6 months for LoyaltyOS).

Key products featured: **LoyaltyOS** (loyalty marketing platform), **UEngage** (CDP + marketing automation), **UReach** (AI CRM Agent), **UAgent** (AI Agent Builder), **Promotion Engine**, **UCDP**, **CRM Next**, **Saleswork AI**, **UPOS**, and **Headless Commerce**.

## Key facts

- Utop = member company of FPT Corporation; backed by FPT + SBI Holdings
- Brand umbrella: **FPT CX Suite** (contains all Utop products)
- Certifications: ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II
- Available on: Microsoft Azure Marketplace
- Deployment: On-premises or SaaS; microservices architecture
- Deployment speed: 4–6 months for LoyaltyOS
- Target verticals: banking, retail, telecom, insurance, FMCG
