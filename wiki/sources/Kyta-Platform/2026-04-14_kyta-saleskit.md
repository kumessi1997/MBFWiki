## Summary

21-slide English saleskit for Kyta Platform. Covers current challenges (delayed approvals, compliance/legal risks, fragmented data, high admin costs), pain points Kyta solves, full platform introduction, AI features (Kyta Intelligent), competitive differentiators, certification & security, key clients, and one detailed case study.

Key message: Kyta is an "All-in-One Platform for Digital Operations" — from end-to-end document management and secure e-signatures to no-code workflow automation, centralized storage, smart document drafting, and personalized workspaces.

The case study features an unnamed Vietnamese dairy company (top 50 globally, 40 units) that used Kyta to digitize all contract signing during COVID-19: contract time reduced from 5 days to <1 day; 50,000 contracts and documents executed on the system.

## Key facts

- **Tagline:** "Innovation Meets Digitalization — Paperless, Standardization, Mobility, Digitalization, Intelligent"
- **8 platform capabilities:** End-to-End Document Management, Secure E-Signatures & Authentication, No-Code Workflow Automation, Centralized Storage, Intuitive Form Builder, Smart Document Drafting, Comprehensive Reporting, Personalized Workspace
- **AI layer (Kyta Intelligent):** AI-powered drafting, AI-driven request approval support, AI-based regex for form fields, AI task analytics, AI email template writing, AI text information extraction, AI knowledge search
- **Competitive differentiators:** No-code automation, AI-powered, Multi-cloud deployment, Cross-department templates
- **Security:** ISO 9001:2008, ISO 27001, CMMI3; multi-layer (Firewall, WAF, Data Encryption, Multi-Tenancy, Pentest); PCI DSS (FPT Smart Cloud); eKYC FaceID Biometric cert (Tayllorcox ISO 30107)
- **Deployment:** FPT Cloud (primary); AWS/Google Cloud compatible; private cloud; fast deployment; FPT Smart Cloud document storage
- **Case study:** Unnamed top-50 global dairy company (VN); 40 units; 5 days → <1 day contract signing; 50,000 contracts signed
- **Named clients (logo slide):** Fresenius Kabi, Takahata, Ford, Hoya, Nestlé, Denso, Henkel, Toyota FS, KFC, Samsung, Sony, CP Group, Goertek, AEON, SSI, Sonion, Elanco, Prudential, GSK, Mirae Asset, AstraZeneca, Cargill, NISHIO, Nagase, Shopee, Shinhan Securities, JACCS, TMF, Credit Connect, and others
- **Process recommendations across 6 domains:** HR, Business, Finance & Accounting, Production, Administrative, Legal
- **Korea compliance:** Korean Electronic Signature Act + PIPA-compliant eKYC (indicating international expansion intent)
