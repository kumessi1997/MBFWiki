## Summary

5-page English brochure for Kyta Platform, FPT IS's digital identification and agreement management platform. Positions Kyta as an all-in-one solution covering three core products: Kyta ALM (Agreement Lifecycle Management), Kyta Signature (e-signature), and Kyta Gate (asset verification & management).

The brochure emphasizes legal compliance (Ministry of Public Security, Ministry of Information & Communications, Ministry of Industry and Trade), advanced AI-powered eKYC, and no-code/low-code workflow creation. Claims 4,500+ business customers. Reference logos visible: AEON, Samsung, Uniqlo, KFC, Unilever, Shopee, Honda, Sabeco.

## Key facts

- **3 products:** Kyta ALM, Kyta Signature, Kyta Gate
- **4,500+ businesses** trust Kyta Platform
- **6 target industries:** Finance/Banking, Insurance, Education, Manufacturing & Supply Chain, Real Estate, Retail & E-commerce
- **Legal compliance (Vietnam):** Ministry of Public Security (identity), MIC (digital signatures), Ministry of Industry and Trade (document certification)
- **Technology stack:** No-code/low-code, TSA + LTV digital signatures, eKYC (NFC + ID Check + MPS cross-check), AI (chatbot/voicebot, decision support), Big Data analytics
- **Deployment:** Cloud-based on FPT Cloud; AWS/Google Cloud compatible; private cloud option; 24/7 support
- **Website:** kyta.fpt.com
