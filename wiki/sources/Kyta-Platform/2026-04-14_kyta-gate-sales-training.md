## Summary

Vietnamese internal sales training deck focused specifically on **Kyta Gate** — the identity verification and electronic agreement module of Kyta Platform. Covers pain points, solution overview, case studies (F83 and OCB), technology differentiators, deployment model, and sales team commitments.

Kyta Gate is positioned as an end-to-end identification and agreement platform (giải pháp toàn trình định danh & giao kết điện tử). Key differentiator: unlike fragmented point solutions (finance-specific or securities-specific tools), Kyta Gate is a single platform covering any business's identification and agreement workflow from A to Z.

Uses a "LEGO block" metaphor: Kyta Gate's submodules can be quickly assembled and customized for any industry vertical without modification.

## Key facts

- **Full submodule list (Kyta Gate):**
  - eAccount: account & permission management
  - eTask: task management
  - eForm: form creation & configuration
  - eFlow: workflow automation
  - eRequest: business operation management
  - eWriter: online document drafting with data-driven templates
  - FPT.eContract: electronic signing anytime/anywhere
  - FPT.CeCA: document authentication
  - eConDrive: centralized data sync & storage
  - eCatalogue: system catalog management
  - eID: individual & corporate identity verification
  - eLMS: loan file, loan account, and disbursement management
- **5 technology differentiators:**
  1. An tâm pháp lý (Legal compliance): Ministry of Industry & Trade green tick on signed documents
  2. Định danh chính xác 100% (100% identity accuracy): eKYC, IDCheck, AI cross-check with Ministry of Public Security
  3. Linh hoạt tạo quy trình (Flexible process creation): drag-and-drop, customize available modules
  4. Tiết kiệm 80% chi phí vận hành (80% operating cost reduction); tiết kiệm 90% thời gian xử lý (90% processing time reduction)
  5. 100% online on one platform
- **Case study 1 — F83** (personal finance platform, installment loans): Kyta Gate applied to installment loan flow: create profile → appraisal → sign & authenticate → disbursement
- **Case study 2 — OCB bank**: Kyta Gate for electronic document management: create e-document → approve → sign & authenticate → manage & report
- **Target use cases by industry:**
  - Banking: corporate/individual onboarding
  - Consumer finance companies: pledge/consumer loan → contract signing
  - Insurance: contract signing, claims processing
  - Securities companies: corporate/individual onboarding
  - Chain/agency business model: agent agreement signing, JV, other contracts
- **Deployment:** Cloud (FPT Smart Cloud); fast deployment; automated document storage; 24/7 support team
