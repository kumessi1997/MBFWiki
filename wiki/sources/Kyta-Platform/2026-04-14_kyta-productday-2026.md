## Summary

Internal Product Day 2026 deck for Kyta Platform (Vietnamese). The most strategically rich Kyta document — includes competitive differentiation table vs. international and domestic players, detailed target audience segmentation, deal pipeline data, revenue targets, and 2026 product roadmap.

Key framing: Kyta evolved from FPT.eContract (e-contract only) → standardization/DMS → No-code/Low-code/AI → Kyta Platform (iPaaS — Integration Platform as a Service). Positioning: "Một nền tảng, vạn ứng dụng" (One platform, unlimited applications).

## Key facts

**Product evolution origin story:**
- FPT.eContract (e-contract + document management) → process standardization → low-code/no-code + AI → Kyta Platform (connect, integrate, customize, automate = digital platform)

**Core value proposition:** Intelligent document management + Workflow + No-code/Low-code + AI + iPaaS integration layer

**KPIs claimed:**
- 80% operating cost reduction (printing, storage, logistics)
- 90% processing/approval time reduction
- 100% data and workflows moved online
- >4,800 enterprises and organizations

**Named reference clients (from deal pipeline):**
- Q1/2025 go-live: Sumi-Hanel (1B VND, 4w), CapitaLand (1B, 4w), ETS (1.2B, 4w), Lof (1.2B, 8w)
- Larger deals: Sabeco (10B, 6m), Luxshare ICT (10B, 8w), Yusen Logistics (2.5B, 8w), PTSC (2B, 8w)
- Pipeline: PTSC M&C (12B), DBV Insurance (7B)
- **Total contracts signed: 70B VND**
- **Pipeline target: 300B VND**

**Revenue targets:**
- 600B VND trong 3 năm tới (next 3 years)
- 1,000B VND năm 2030
- Revenue mix: Government 40%, Large domestic enterprises 40%, Mass 10%, Global 10%

**Competitive differentiation table (Kyta vs. BOAT International vs. Domestic SaaS):**

| Criteria | Kyta Platform | Camunda/SAP/Microsoft | Base/1Office/Lark |
|---|---|---|---|
| Workflow Orchestration | Very High: drag-drop, low/no-code, multi-system | Very High: microservice but deep coding | Medium: good for simple internal approvals |
| AI + IDP | High: Vietnamese legal AI, fraud detection | Medium-High: strong AI Agents, lacks VN legal AI | Low-Medium: basic gen AI only |
| eKYC | Excellent: built-in, CCCD NFC, MPS check | Not available: 3rd-party API needed | Very Limited: username/password only |
| E-Signature & VN Legal | Excellent: TSA, LTV, CeCA | Weak: no VN legal authentication | Medium: internal e-sign, not synced with national system |
| Deployment Speed | Very Fast: flexible B2B/B2C high volume | Very Slow: heavy, large budget | Fast: good for SME internal mgmt |

**Target audiences (4 primary):**
1. **Government (Chính phủ số):** digitize admin, manage citizen records; reference: Bộ Nội vụ
2. **Finance/Banking (Tài chính - Ngân hàng):** automate credit approvals, risk & compliance; reference: expanding sector
3. **Real Estate (Bất động sản):** contract management, legal files; reference: CapitaLand (CLD)
4. **Manufacturing (Sản xuất):** supply chain optimization, factory automation; reference: Yusen Logistics, Lof, ETS, Sumi, Sabeco, PTSC

**Sales guidance (4 pitching angles):**
1. "AI Native" + Low-code/No-code
2. "All-in-one" BOAT platform ("Hệ điều hành doanh nghiệp")
3. Security & legal compliance (eKYC/IDCheck verification)
4. Proven with "Ông lớn" (big enterprises): SABECO, Bộ Tư Pháp

**2026 Product Roadmap:**
- Q2 2026: App Builder — users can package modules into their own branded app
- Q3 2026: AI Agent — AI embedded in every feature and submodule
- Q4 2026: AI Operating System — AI multi-agent system coordinating all platform activities

**Awards (full list):**
Giải Vàng iKhiến 2025, Giải Đồng Stevie Awards, Sao Khue 2021, Vietnam Digital Transformation Awards 2021 & 2023 & 2025, Top 10 Make in Vietnam 2021, International Business Awards 2023
