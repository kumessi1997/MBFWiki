## Summary

Two internal sales training documents. The pricing sheet (BÁO GIÁ, 2023, 1 page) is the official FPT price list for UBot Invoice, UBot Matching, and UBot ePayment. The Q&A document (50 questions, 2 pages) is a structured knowledge test covering the UBot ecosystem — product features, technical constraints, invoice validity logic, and handling edge cases — intended to certify sales staff.

## Key facts

**Pricing (VND, 2023):**
- One-time setup fee: 5,000,000 VND per module (Invoice / Matching / ePayment)
- UBot Invoice license: 1,500 VND/tx (0–1,999 tx) → 500 VND/tx (500,000+ tx)
- UBot Matching license: 1,000 VND/tx (0–1,999 tx) → 300 VND/tx (500,000+ tx)
- UBot ePayment license: 50,000 VND/user/month (small tiers) → 30,000 VND/user/month (large tiers)
- Warranty: at customer's site; duration = full UBot subscription period
- Customization outside standard features: requires separate analysis and quotation

**Q&A key facts:**
- UBot is based on **RPA** (Robotic Process Automation) technology
- Currently **3,000–4,000 customers**
- No mobile app; web-only; requires internet + account
- Invoice validity checks: **7 criteria** (seller active, tax code valid, no duplicate, buyer name, buyer tax code, buyer address, digital signature serial valid)
- Paper invoice handling: OCR scanning (user scans/photos and uploads)
- Handles XML invoices from GDT (Vietnam Tax Authority)
- Email auto-forward: supported; frequency configurable (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Can retroactively process invoices predating UBot registration
- Storage: Cloud; full period; on termination, exports all data (PDF, XML, images) to customer
- Unlimited concurrent users; unlimited businesses/MST per account
- Supports granular permission management (per-user, per-action)
- Deployment: immediately after contract + payment
- UBot is NOT an accounting system; outputs data for import into PMKT/accounting software
- Purchase process bots: **4** (Invoice, Matching, ePayment, Statement Checking)
- UBot Matching does partial auto-accounting: maps item codes, tax codes, account codes, warehouse codes, cost centers when Matching Data feature enabled
