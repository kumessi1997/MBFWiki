## Summary

Two complementary UBot brochures covering the same product from different angles. The Introduction deck (20 slides, Vietnamese) is a solution-focused sales pitch covering 4 core purchase-process bots (Invoice, Matching, ePayment, Statement Checking) with before/after comparisons and detailed feature breakdowns. The 2023 Brochure (12 pages) is a consumer-facing product sheet reinforcing the same 4 bots, plus covering UBot Meeting (shareholder meeting solution), the full ecosystem wheel (Finance/Front-office/Operation/Management), full client roster, and awards.

Both establish UBot as Vietnam's first comprehensive RPAaaS ecosystem, developed by akaBot–FPT, targeting 3,000+ SME and mid-market customers across 14 industries. The core value proposition is complete digitization of the Finance & Accounting department's purchase and sales cycles, delivered as a cloud service requiring zero infrastructure investment and 15-minute setup.

## Key facts

- UBot = RPAaaS; cloud-only; no mobile app; works on desktop browser + internet
- **3,000+ customers** · **20+ countries** · **14 industries** · **3.5 million invoices processed**
- 4 purchase-process bots: Invoice, Matching, ePayment, Statement Checking
- 5 sales-process bots: Matching, Sales Order, Sales Invoice, Collection, Payment Reminder
- UBot Invoice: 90% automation; 3 min → 30s per invoice; 80% time saved; 100% accuracy
- UBot Matching: 3-way match (PO + GRN + Invoice); result in 2s; 100% accuracy
- UBot ePayment: payment request in 2s; multi-level online approval
- UBot Statement Checking: auto bank statement download & AR/AP reconciliation; 80% time saved
- UBot Meeting: AGM solution; FPT.AI eKYC identity check; 70% cost savings
- Setup: 3 steps — Demo → 15-minute setup → 24/7/365 use
- Case studies: Daikin (40K invoices/yr, 130 working days saved), Rạng Đông (15–25% cost saved), Expertis (70% time saved)
