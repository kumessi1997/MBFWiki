## Summary

GeoBase Cloud is FPT IS's **Geographic Information System (GIS) Platform** — a cloud-based solution for managing, processing, and sharing geospatial data. Launched in 2024, it addresses the fragmented, paper-heavy workflows of field engineers, government agencies, and enterprises that deal with spatial data (maps, drawings, land surveys). The platform converts diverse GIS data formats (DWG, DGN, SHP, GML, TAB, GeoJSON, BIM, point clouds, etc.) into centralized, shareable web map services accessible via browser or mobile app.

The saleskit (31 slides) covers overview, deployment process, industry applications, and pricing across three tiers (Lite/Pro/Enterprise SaaS + on-premise). A companion deck — **GeoBase Geospatial Data Viewer** (v0.1, 11 slides, dated 2026-04-16) — introduces a sub-module focused on 3D model and point cloud visualization for construction/engineering teams, with quantified efficiency benefits.

## Key facts

- FPT IS product; copyright 2024 FPT IS
- Online platform: upload, manage, access map data without complex standardization
- 8 key functions including multi-format data conversion, mobile data collection, 3D/BIM/Digital Twin integration, multi-national coordinate systems (VN-2000, WGS-84, HN72, UTM, Lambert-93, etc.)
- Mobile app available on App Store and CH Play (Lite & Pro versions)
- Saves 10%–20% operational time and costs
- Adheres to OGC service standards; supports rapid custom app development on platform
- Deployment ≤ 30 days (on-premise); SLA: Level 1 critical response ≤ 1 working hour
- **Geospatial Data Viewer module**: saves 60% field survey time, 40% productivity gain, 50% hardware cost reduction, 95% measurement accuracy improvement, 70% faster report preparation
