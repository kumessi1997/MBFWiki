## Summary

Vietnamese-language product page clipped from fpteid.com/vi/san-pham. Short marketing overview of FPT eID's four solutions and supporting features. Corroborates the November 2025 saleskit; adds no new technical data but provides the public-facing product narrative and a condensed description of the 6-step usage workflow.

## Key facts

**Four solutions (public page description):**

| Solution | Description |
|---|---|
| **eKYC** | AI-powered individual identity verification; 98% accuracy; anti-deepfake; API/SDK for Web/iOS/Android; CCCD face match |
| **FPT.IDCheck** | NFC chip CCCD authentication against MoPS (Bộ Công An) database; RAR-signed data integrity check; multi-platform |
| **eKYB** | Business onboarding; OCR of 5 business license types; cross-check with national business portal + tax authority; FPT.CA integration |
| **FaceID** | Face recognition + liveness detection + blacklist search; 512 features in 0.1s; handles poor lighting/blur/masks |

**6-step usage workflow:**
1. Capture both sides of CCCD
2. NFC scan → verify with RAR (Trung tâm nghiên cứu và ứng dụng dữ liệu dân cư)
3. Capture selfie
4. Face comparison against stored data
5. Profile completion

**Platform features:**
- Multi-platform: iOS, Android, Web App
- NFC: ICAO standard chip extraction + National Data Center cross-check
- Face matching + liveness detection
- OCR for business registration documents

**Awards:**
- Ministry of Public Security D06 certification (Đề Án D06)
- Vietnam Digital Transformation Award 2023
