## Summary

VertZéro is FPT IS's ISO 14064-1 compliant carbon accounting software, positioned at the intersection of enterprise ESG compliance and Vietnam's accelerating regulatory push toward net-zero. The platform provides end-to-end GHG inventory management — covering Scope 1 (direct), Scope 2 (purchased energy), and Scope 3 (value chain) emissions — with automated data collection, multi-standard reporting, and a Marginal Abatement Cost Curve (MACC) tool for decarbonization planning.

The saleskit frames VertZéro against three macro drivers: CBAM (the EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism taxing carbon-intensive imports from 2026), Vietnam's Decree 06/2022/ND-CP mandating GHG inventories for large emitters, and Power Plan VIII phasing out coal. These pressures are sharpest for Vietnamese manufacturers exporting to Europe and large corporates needing ESG disclosure.

The product's differentiation centers on its emission factor database (60,000+ factors spanning IPCC, US EPA, DEFRA, Ecoinvent, and custom enterprise libraries), its native compliance output for CBAM/CSRD/ISSB/CDP/GRI/SASB frameworks, and a partner ecosystem that includes TÜV Rheinland (verification), Carbon EX Japan (carbon credits and renewable energy certificates), and FPT Digital (ESG advisory).

Deployment is structured as a 3-phase engagement: Preparation (Weeks 1–3: scoping, training, baseline data collection), Deployment & Integration (Weeks 4–10: system setup, API integrations, reporting configuration), and Operations (ongoing monitoring and annual reporting cycle).

## Key facts

- **Compliance standards**: ISO 14064-1:2011, GHG Protocol, IPCC, CBAM, CSRD, ISSB, SBTi, CDP, GRI, SASB
- **Emission factor database**: 60,000+ factors (IPCC, US EPA, DEFRA, Ecoinvent, custom enterprise libraries)
- **Decarbonization templates**: 120+ project templates covering energy efficiency, renewable energy, process changes, and logistics
- **Scope coverage**: Scope 1 (direct combustion/processes), Scope 2 (purchased electricity/heat), Scope 3 (15 upstream/downstream categories including supply chain, employee commuting, product use)
- **Key tools**: Marginal Abatement Cost Curve (MACC), SBTi compliance checker, Vendor Emissions Management (Tier 1 & Tier 2), Employee Engagement Platform (commute tracking app), Procurement Emission Management
- **Integration**: Automated data pull via supplier ERP/SCM APIs; connects to energy meters, IoT sensors, and procurement systems
- **Website**: https://vertzero.eco/en/
- **Market context**: Vietnam = 3rd largest GHG emitter in Southeast Asia; CBAM Phase 2 (2026) covers cement, steel, aluminum, fertilizers, electricity, hydrogen — key Vietnamese export sectors
