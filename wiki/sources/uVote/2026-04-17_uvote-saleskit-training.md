## Summary

Four UVote sales and training documents: an EN leaflet (4 pages), a VI leaflet (4 pages, identical content), a Training for AM deck (20 slides), and a Q&A knowledge document (20 questions, Vietnamese). Together they constitute the complete commercial picture of UVote — product positioning, system architecture, pricing, competitive landscape, legal context, and operational details.

UVote is positioned as Vietnam's leading AGM & e-voting solution, powered by akaBot–FPT. Its vision is to become the #1 Voting & Election Platform in Asia-Pacific. The product serves the full AGM lifecycle: pre-meeting setup (invitations, shareholder list, proxy management) → live meeting (eKYC check-in, livestream, Q&A chat) → voting (real-time, per-item or batch) → reporting (auto-export in 2 seconds). Key differentiators are the 48-hour end-to-end setup time, legal compliance with Vietnam Enterprise Law 2014, and proven scale (1M+ shareholders served, VN30 client roster).

## Key facts

**Scale & market:**
- **1,000,000+ shareholders** served to date
- Vietnam AGM market: 2,114 listed companies (HOSE 417 + HNX 342 + Upcom 855 + OTC 500)
- Global e-voting market: USD $1.33B (2021) → $3.47B (2028), CAGR 12.7%

**System architecture:**
- 3 nodes: **Portal** (UVote Operators) · **Admin** (Issuing Organization) · **AGM** (Participants / Shareholders)
- Technology stack: Streaming Service, Online Payment, E-sign, Gamification, Chatbot, Blockchain
- Identity verification: eKYC (FPT.AI) or OTP SMS or QR Code

**Key capabilities for organizers (Q8):**
- Shareholder list finalization; auto invitation email; proxy declaration
- Online election/voting; livestream viewing; Q&A chat
- Realtime attendance rate & per-item vote tally
- Auto report export: shareholder verification report, vote count results, meeting minutes & resolutions

**Voting rules (per Q&A):**
- 3 login methods: QR Code · OTP · eKYC (CMND/CCCD/MSDN/Passport/ownership registration number)
- Shareholders can delegate to multiple proxies (each proxy gets own account)
- Vote early (before deadline); vote changes allowed before deadline; system records final state at close
- Cumulative voting (bầu dồn phiếu) and ballot splitting supported
- Disconnection only affects uncast votes; past votes preserved
- One shareholder can attend multiple AGMs simultaneously on one device
- Only meeting chair can see running vote results during voting; chair may share access with committee

**Legal basis:**
- Online AGM legally valid per Article 140.2(c), Enterprise Law 2014 (Luật Doanh nghiệp 2014)
- Annual AGM required within 4 months of fiscal year-end (before Apr 30; extendable to Jun 30)
- Quorum measured at open; vote tally based on shares actually present at voting time
