## Summary

A 2-page VI product brochure for FPT.PetroInvoice — a specialized e-invoice solution designed exclusively for the petroleum/fuel retail sector. The brochure covers the legal mandate driving demand, two deployment solutions (AI camera-based vs. electronic display replacement), pricing, a named client case study, and contact details.

FPT.PetroInvoice addresses the Government Directive 1284/CĐ-TTg requiring all petrol stations to issue e-invoices **per transaction starting 12/2023**. Traditional hardware upgrades cost 400M–1B VND per station; FPT provides a software-first alternative starting at **2 triệu VND/station** and **50đ/invoice** (vs. 500đ/invoice on standard TVAN).

## Key facts

**Legal driver:**
- Directive 1284/CĐ-TTg: all petrol stations (cửa hàng xăng dầu) must issue e-invoices per retail transaction from 12/2023
- Without FPT: 400M–1B VND per station to upgrade hardware + software per pump
- With FPT: from 2 triệu VND setup + 50đ/invoice (10× cheaper per invoice)

**Solution 1 — FPT.PetroInvoice AI**
- For: all fuel pump types (mọi loại trụ bơm)
- Deploy: **1 day per petrol station**
- Technology: Smart Camera reads transaction amount from pump's electronic display → sends to FPT.PetroInvoice → auto-issues e-invoice per GDT regulation
- Handles both customers who request invoice and those who don't
- Integrates with existing petroleum management software (daily data sync + audit)
- QR Code dynamic payment for customers on mobile (TPBank, NAPAS, VIETGR, VNPAY shown)
- Auto-alerts for abnormal invoice status via email/SMS
- Already deployed in Nam Dinh and Hai Duong provinces

**Solution 2 — Electronic Display Unit Replacement (Thay thế bộ chỉ thị điện tử)**
- For: pumps without built-in data transmission capability
- Deploy: **1–2 days per petrol station**
- Replaces old electronic display; new unit transmits fuel transaction data to centralized collection system at station
- **Case study:** FPT IS + Nam Duong Technical Co. deployed for **Saigon Petro** (Công ty TNHH MTV Dầu khí TP. HCM) since 2022

**Pricing:**
- Setup: from **2,000,000 VND/station** (vs. 400M–1B without FPT)
- Per invoice: from **50 VND** (vs. 500 VND standard)
