## Summary

This is the ArchiveNex English product brochure (2 pages), presenting ArchiveNex as FPT IS's comprehensive electronic archiving solution for organizations managing long-term and permanent records. The product positions itself around the OAIS ISO 14721:2012 standard — the international reference model for archival information systems — combined with AI, interactive 3D archives, and compliance with Vietnam's revised Archiving Law.

The brochure reveals ArchiveNex sits within a broader **FPT IS document management and archiving ecosystem** alongside D-Office, iSOMA, GeoBase, eLearning, and [[akaVerse]] — covering the full lifecycle from document management through digitization, submission, archive management, and virtual exhibition/training.

## Key facts

**Product definition:**
- ArchiveNex = comprehensive electronic archiving solution
- Based on OAIS ISO 14721:2012 standard (Open Archival Information System)
- Ensures compliance with Vietnam's revised Archiving Law
- Guarantees long-term or permanent archival value via evidence-creation methods

**Key features (6):**
1. Secure and permanent archiving
2. Customized comprehensive solution
3. Efficient exploitation and use
4. User-friendly, future-proof development
5. Promoting the value of digital heritage
6. Meeting standards and legal requirements

**Product values (6):**
1. OAIS international standard — ISO 14721
2. Application of Artificial Intelligence (AI)
3. Comprehensive solution for multiple fields
4. Ability to integrate and expand
5. Interactive 3D digital archive
6. Successfully deployed at high-level state organizations

**OAIS architecture (3-layer):**
- **S.I.P** (Submission Information Package) — ingest and submission layer
- **A.I.P** (Archival Information Package) — storage and preservation layer
- **D.I.P** (Dissemination Information Package) — access and retrieval layer

**3 core services:**
1. Digitization
2. Processing and classifying digital documents
3. Standardization of archived data

**Dual archiving scope:**
- *Current Archiving*: planning, receiving, editing/organizing, retention management, destruction, publishing, integration with provincial/national historical archives
- *Historical Archiving*: collection, receipt, appraisal, permanent preservation planning, digital document preservation, publishing, integration with National Historical Archives

**International certifications:** ISO 27000, CMMI Level 3, ISO 20000, Bureau Veritas

> [!NOTE]
> ArchiveNex shows CMMI Level 3 certification — while parent [[FPT IS]] holds CMMI Level 5. This likely reflects the certification of the ArchiveNex unit specifically, not the full FPT IS entity.

**Flagship success story — Ministry of Home Affairs (Cục Văn Thư và Lưu Trữ Nhà Nước):**
- 20.3 million digitized document pages
- 300 submission sources
- 5 archive centers
- Built centralized electronic archiving system per OAIS ISO 14721:2012
- Became model system for provincial historical archive centers nationwide

**Target sectors (4):**
1. State agencies and State-owned enterprises
2. Archival agencies and organizations preserving cultural heritage
3. Education institutions
4. Natural resources, environment, and construction

**FPT IS document management ecosystem (cross-product matrix):**
