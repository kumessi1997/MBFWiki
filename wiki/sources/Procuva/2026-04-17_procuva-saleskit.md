## Summary

PROCUVA is FPT IS's Procurement & Bidding Management System, positioned under the tagline "Empower Smart Procurement." The 25-slide EN saleskit covers the full Procure-to-Pay (P2P) lifecycle from budget control through supplier management, bidding, contract execution, and payment. The product targets enterprises with manual procurement pain points — particularly in manufacturing, distribution, finance, and banking verticals.

The deck opens with a strong market case: procurement software delivers 98% reduction in paperwork, 99% reduction in errors, 0.2–2% annual cost savings, 10x productivity increase, and 92% spending control (cited from McKinsey, BCG, EY, and FPT's own implementation data). PROCUVA's product philosophy is "Ceaseless Improvement" — a structured 6-step improvement cycle (Identify Requirements → Analyze Data → Define Scope → Develop Plan → Implement Plan → Evaluate Results) deployed alongside the customer.

The saleskit closes with 5 unnamed customer case studies across banking, manufacturing, FMCG, and retail verticals showing concrete ROI: a joint-stock bank (18B VND annual savings, 10x speed), a leading brewery (10–15B VND savings, 50% time reduction, >5,000 online bidders), a stationery corporation (>100K orders/year, 100% sales order integration), the largest F&B corporation (>10,000 active suppliers, 80% faster supplier selection), and a leading retail distribution chain (>200 automated stores, 10B VND savings).

## Key facts

- 4 core modules: Budget Management, Procurement Management, Payment Management, Supplier Portal
- 2-portal system: Internal Portal (procurement team) + Supplier Portal (vendors)
- 8 technical advantages including parallel process execution, dynamic authorization & delegation, conditional workflow routing
- Integrates with ERP: SAP, Oracle, BRAVO, Microsoft Dynamics 365
- FPT ecosystem integrations: akaBot, Kyta Platform, FPT Digital Accounting, FPT.eSign, FPT.eInvoice, FPT.eContract
- Strictly compliant with Vietnam Bidding Law; supports multiple bidding methods
- Uses AI and Big Data for supplier recommendations, cost forecasting, and contract analysis
- eGP (electronic Government Procurement) expertise applied to enterprise bidding
- Target industries: Manufacturing, Distribution, Finance, Banking
- Flexible Pricing Policy — customizable options
