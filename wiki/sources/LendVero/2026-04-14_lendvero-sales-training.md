## Summary

Vietnamese-language internal sales training deck (v2.0) for LendVero. Provides market context for Vietnam's consumer finance sector, CTTC (Consumer Finance Company) painpoints, and product positioning for both LOS and Debt Collection. Primary audience is FPT IS account managers pitching to CTTCs.

Key market framing: Vietnam's consumer finance market is underpenetrated relative to regional peers — consumer credit/GDP ratio of 27.17% vs. higher ratios in Thailand, Malaysia, and developed markets. This creates a growth narrative: CTTCs that modernize now will capture disproportionate share as the market matures.

The training deck mirrors the English saleskits in content but adds competitive framing and internal sales guidance not present in customer-facing materials.

## Key facts

- Target buyer: Consumer Finance Companies (CTTC) — 16 licensed in Vietnam
- Vietnam consumer debt: 2.58 trillion VND total, representing ~22% of the credit system
- Vietnam consumer credit/GDP: **27.17%** — low vs. regional peers (growth opportunity thesis)
- 5 CTTC painpoints: competitive pressure from banks, limited capital access, compliance burden, risk management challenges, legacy/fragmented IT systems
- LOS benefits emphasized: time savings (automation), accuracy (eKYC), safety (audit trails), end-to-end coverage
- Debt Collection benefits: omnichannel outreach, DPD-based prioritization, field mobile app, legal case management
- Same 8-step LOS process and 6-stage collection process as English saleskit
- Internal sales guidance: lead with market growth story, then CTTC painpoints, then LendVero solution
