## Summary

LendVero is [[FPT IS]]'s end-to-end digital lending platform targeting consumer finance companies (CTTCs) in Vietnam. The brochure introduces the platform at a high level, presenting two core modules — [[Loan Origination System]] (LOS) and Debt Collection — as an integrated solution to modernize the full loan lifecycle from application to recovery.

The document positions LendVero against fragmented legacy systems common at smaller and mid-size finance companies, emphasizing speed (automated processing), accuracy (100% eKYC), and end-to-end coverage. The flagship case study is [[JACCS]] (JIVF — Japan Consumer Credit Group's Vietnam arm), where LendVero delivered a 70% reduction in loan processing time and 100% eKYC verification accuracy.

## Key facts

- Two modules: **LOS** (Loan Origination System) and **Debt Collection**
- 100% eKYC accuracy claim using OCR, NFC, Liveness, and Ministry of Public Security (MPS) verification
- 70% processing time reduction at JACCS/JIVF (flagship case study)
- Target segment: Consumer Finance Companies (CTTC) licensed in Vietnam
- Fully customizable BPM workflow (jBPM/Camunda engine)
- Mobile-first for both sales agents (LOS) and field collectors (Debt Collection)
