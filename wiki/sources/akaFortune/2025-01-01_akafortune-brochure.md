## Summary

This is the two-page English brochure for **akaFortune**, published by FPT IS. It presents akaFortune as "Vietnam's Pioneering All-In-One Financial Advisory Platform" — a platform designed for financial advisors and wealth management firms offering investment products (stocks, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, margin).

The brochure covers the platform's six capability pillars, full module/engine/data architecture, and three competitive advantage themes. No client case studies or pricing data appear in this document.

> [!NOTE]
> No publication date on the brochure. Approximated as 2025 based on folder context. Treat date as uncertain.

## Key facts

- Publisher: FPT IS Company Limited (product under FPT IS Banking & Finance vertical)
- Self-described as "Vietnam's Pioneering All-In-One Financial Advisory Platform"
- Target buyers: financial advisors, wealth management firms, brokerages, banks offering investment advisory
- Supported products: Stocks, Bonds, Mutual Fund, ETF, Margin

**Platform architecture (3 layers):**

| Layer | Components |
|---|---|
| **Modules** | Investment Advisory, Investment Information Package, Copy Trading, Investment Trust, Marketplace (Investors ↔ Advisors), Digital Investment Community Platform, Client Management, Sales Management |
| **Engines** | Investment Advisory, Performance Index, Copy Trading, Incentive, Smart Placing Order, Rating, Portfolio Optimization, Backtesting |
| **Data** | Client, Investment Portfolio, Price History, Market Data |

**Six capability pillars:**
1. **Engagement Platform** — unified view of all services and investment portfolios; transparent real-time information
2. **Multi-Services Platform** — integrates wide product range; comprehensive advisory support; intuitive UX
3. **Sales Management Platform** — digitizes sales ops and performance management; expands digital sales channels
4. **Investment Community Platform** — connects investors and financial experts; real-time discussion; professional community
5. **Portfolio Construction Tools** — Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT) optimization; historical backtesting; ongoing adjustments
6. **Risk Analytics Engine** — Value-at-Risk (VaR) model; Max Drawdown and Sharpe Ratio; price-fluctuation analysis

**Competitive advantage themes:**
- Tailor-made for financial investment (portfolio-centric, comprehensive, automated)
- Maximize operational efficiency (digitize sales, accelerate channel growth)
- Deliver excellence at competitive cost (hybrid architecture, fast deployment)
