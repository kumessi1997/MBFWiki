## Summary

This is the Azladin English product brochure (7 pages, 2024), presenting Azladin as FPT IS's **Digital Healthcare Platform** — a cloud SaaS infrastructure product that unifies hospital and healthcare systems across Vietnam through standards-based interoperability. Unlike AZINSU (which focuses on insurance claims), Azladin sits at the clinical and operational layer: patient records, medical imaging, clinical decision support, and health information exchange across facilities.

The brochure reveals Azladin's customer base as **300+ healthcare facilities across Vietnam** (explicitly matching the figure cited in AZINSU brochure), spanning public hospitals, private hospitals, military hospitals, clinics, and national healthcare corporations.

## Key facts

**Product definition:**
- Azladin = Digital Healthcare Platform
- Tagline: "Connected services · Better insights · Better care"
- Target users: Patients, Doctors, Partners, Staff

**4 value propositions:**
1. **Coordinated Care** — Intuitive digital tools for personalized, connected care
2. **Care Team Empowerment** — Seamless collaboration with advanced technology
3. **Efficiency Workflow** — Drive efficiency and accuracy in clinical workflows
4. **Enhanced Patient Outcome** — Better-informed clinical decisions

**How Azladin works (4 pillars):**
1. **Cloud SaaS Platform** — Scalable digital and data solutions
2. **Interoperability** — Seamless Health Information Exchange
3. **Secure Systems** — Reliable, resilient, user-centric designs
4. **Digital Transformation** — Supporting providers in innovation

**5 key features:**
- Real-time insights for decisions
- Smooth and user-friendly
- Timely and relevant data
- Secure and reliable
- Integrated healthcare ecosystem

**6 functional domains:**

| Domain | Components |
|---|---|
| **01 Clinical Data Sharing** | Patient demographics, encounters, allergies, history, lab results, radiology reports/images, medications, immunizations, diagnoses, ECG, spirometry, ophthalmology, vital signs, clinical documents, appointments |
| **02 Registries & eMPI Service** | Master Patient Index; Organization Registry; Practitioner Registry |
| **03 Terminology** | Snomed-CT · LOINC · ICD10 · HL7 FHIR |
| **04 Services** | SMART-on-FHIR, SMART Health Link, International Patient Summary; Clinical Decision Support; Shared Health Record; Data Analytics; Clinical Evidence Analysis (evidence-based medicine) |
| **05 Integration** | HIS/EMR, LIS, RIS/PACs; Mobile apps for doctors/nurses; KIOSK; Website; Appointments; Social Networks; CRM |
| **06 Repositories** | Clinical Data Repository; Document Data Repository; Imaging Data Repository; Data Analytics Repository; Data Research Repository |

**Deployment method — 2-layer governance framework:**
- *Interoperability Governance*: Process + Semantic + Technology Interoperability → Integrated Health Service Governance
- *Integrated Health Service Governance*: Public Health Services + Business Intelligence + Clinical Research, built on Master Data Management + Clinical Data Repository + Open Data + Health Services

4 interoperability principles: **Security · Privacy · Accessibility · Standardization**
