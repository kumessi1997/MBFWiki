## Summary

A 126-page internal product catalog from **FPT Corporation** (Vietnam's largest IT company), covering the full 2025 B2B software portfolio under the MBF (Market Business Function) classification. Written in both Vietnamese and English. Organized in two dimensions: by **functional category** (what the product does) and by **vertical industry** (which sector it serves).

The catalog is clearly a sales and marketing tool — each product entry includes a 1-page summary, brief description, contact channels, major customers, and links to brand guidelines, brochures, and sales kits. The intended audience is the internal sales force.

## Notable flagship products

- **[[akaBot]]** — RPA & hyperautomation platform; 3,900+ customers, 21 countries, 10 partners. Saves 80% processing time, reduces staff costs 30–50%.
- **FPT.eInvoice** — Electronic invoice solution; one of the first authorized by Vietnam Tax Authority
- **FPT.EagleEye** — Cybersecurity monitoring platform (mSOC, malBot, PCI, VA modules)
- **FPT Semiconductor (Fsemi)** — Chip design subsidiary; one of Vietnam's pioneers in semiconductor design
- **akaMES** — Manufacturing execution system for pharma, steel/aluminum, healthcare devices, fluid/chemical, electronics
