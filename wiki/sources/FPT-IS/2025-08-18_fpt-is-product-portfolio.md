## Summary

This is FPT IS's official product portfolio deck (August 2025), presenting the full range of technology products and solutions offered by FPT Information System Company Limited — a subsidiary of FPT Corporation established in 1994. FPT IS positions itself as a full-spectrum enterprise software provider with 2,900+ clients, 150+ in Vietnam's VNR500 ranking, and 100+ global clients. The company holds CMMI Dev 3.0 Maturity Level 5 certification and ISO 9001 / 27001 / 20000 quality standards.

The portfolio is organized along two axes: **8 product categories** (horizontal, function-based) and **8 vertical industries** (sector-specific deep stacks). This dual structure allows FPT IS to sell both horizontal platform plays (e.g. e-signing, RPA, HR) and sector-specific integrated systems (e.g. core banking, hospital management, e-government). The product count exceeds 70 named products/platforms.

Key brand families: **Volar** (core banking), **Votum** (banking risk/compliance), **Velox** (banking business apps), **akaMES** (manufacturing), **Utop** (CRM/loyalty), **AZINSU** (insurance), and **akaBot/akaVerse/akaMES** (automation/immersive tech).

## Key facts

- FPT IS established: December 31, 1994; 3,700+ employees, 2,400+ experts
- 200+ awards including APICTA, Stevie, Global BankTech, Make in Viet Nam
- 70+ named products across 8 categories and 8 vertical industries
- Flagship scale indicators: FPT.eSign (10M+ users), FPT.eContract (6M+ users, 5,000+ enterprise customers), FPT.eInvoice (70,000+ global customers), akaBot (4,000+ customers in 21 countries), FPT.BSS (100M+ telecom subscribers)
- FPT Culture Tech sub-portfolio targets heritage digitization (DigitalizeX, ArchiveNex, ExperienceX & TourismX)
