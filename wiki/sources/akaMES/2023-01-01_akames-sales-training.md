## Summary

This is the 2023 akaMES sales training document (v0.1, English), the most commercially detailed source in the akaMES folder. It covers the full product positioning, market context, competitive landscape, SWOT analysis, target audience strategy, and — critically — **annual license pricing and one-time module deployment costs**. It is the primary source for akaMES pricing data.

akaMES (website: akames.io) was founded in 2019 and is published by FPT IS. It is positioned as a Digital Manufacturing Platform / Intelligent MOM (Manufacturing Operations Management) solution that controls shopfloor production, unifies cross-department collaboration, and extends ERP with manufacturing-specific capabilities. Global MES market projected to grow from ~$15B (2022) to ~$40B (2030) at CAGR 13.1%.

## SWOT

**Strengths:** Comprehensive, highly customizable; simultaneous multi-factory deployment; AI/ML in real-time production scheduling; qualified consultant team

**Weaknesses:** Limited real-world deployment coverage across manufacturing industries; time needed for customization per customer

**Opportunities:** Global DX wave; "Made by FPT – Make in Vietnam" brand prestige

**Threats:** Many competitors in MES market; AI demands require continuous development

## Target audience (5-stage expansion strategy)

1. Vietnamese manufacturing enterprises + FDI in Vietnam (heavy industry, assembly, steel, wood, garment)
2. Global manufacturing enterprises
3. Multi-factory enterprises across one/many countries
4. Multi-industry corporations (high system integration)
5. Global luxury software — world's leading manufacturing corporations
