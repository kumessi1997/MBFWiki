## Summary

This is akaMES's 2024 introduction deck, positioned as an "Intelligent MOM Solution" (Manufacturing Operations Management). It is the most technically current source for akaMES and introduces new capabilities including **Generative AI in manufacturing** and a four-module solution structure (EMS, OEE & MA, QMS, MES). Compared to the 2023 training document, this deck shows the product's maturity: expanded client roster (30+ clients, 10+ countries), stronger international validation (Yokogawa, IBM, Toray), and explicit AI/GenAI feature integration.

akaMES claims to be **"the first product implementing AI in real-time production operations in the world"** and **"only 1/3 the cost of international solutions."** Technology stack: Industrial IoT + AI/ML/DL + Advanced Analytics + Computer Vision, built on akaMinds IoT data platform.

The deck includes three detailed case studies: Beer Brewing Factory (multi-country), Pharmaceutical Factory (GAMP5), and Steel Manufacturer (PLC+IoT).

**Four core solution modules:**

| Module | Description |
|---|---|
| **EMS** (Energy Management System) | Monitor electricity, water, gas consumption per factory; compare across factories at Head Office |
| **OEE & MA** (Overall Equipment Effectiveness & Maintenance) | Early warning for machine issues via cameras/sensors; root cause analysis; machine efficiency per workstation; predictive maintenance |
| **QMS** (Quality Management System) | Factory-wide quality monitoring; sampling management; SPC charts; abnormal case summaries; synchronized to Head Office in real time |
| **MES** (Manufacturing Execution System) | Production schedule per machine; work order distribution; full bi-directional traceability (raw → finished); ERP integration (SAP, Dynamics, Oracle) |

**Generative AI capabilities:**
- Advanced Scheduling: AI-driven production schedule optimization from historical data
- Dashboard with Chatbot: query production data in natural language for tailored recommendations
- Early Warning: predict maintenance time; adjust plans based on operational reports
- Anomaly Detection: Deep Learning + automated traceability to identify root causes

## Key case studies

**#1 Beer Brewing Factory** (multi-brewery, Asia & Western Europe)
- Challenge: Standardize MES across breweries; ESG energy compliance; OEE/quality KPIs
- Solution: Tier-based brewery categorization; PLC automation; AI + digital tools for operational insights
- Outcome: Increased bottling line utilization; reduced packaging material loss; reduced machine downtime

**#2 Pharmaceutical Factory** (unnamed, Vietnam)
- Challenge: Manual batch production records; error-prone QC documentation; slow scheduling
- Solution: GAMP5-compliant system with production, quality, and warehouse modules; data platform for batch records
- Outcome: GMP-compliant processes; reduced inventory costs; accelerated records and reporting

**#3 Steel Manufacturer** (unnamed, Vietnam)
- Challenge: Semi-automatic lines; disconnected MES data; manual procurement/warehousing
- Solution: MES + PLC integration; mobile + IoT; new warehousing, procurement, and cost accounting modules
- Outcome: 90% reduction in paperwork; improved production reporting; integrated operations and back-office
