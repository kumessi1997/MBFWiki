## Summary

This is the akaMES product portfolio deck (6 pages, 2024), presenting the **akaMES manufacturing software ecosystem** — six packaged software products that cover the full digital manufacturing stack from PLM through MES, maintenance, warehouse, accounting, and data warehouse. It also presents the **cross-industry coverage** (14 industry verticals) and akaMES vertical software solutions.

Key new information vs. other akaMES sources: this document names the five companion products in the akaMES ecosystem that are not akaMES itself — akaPLM, STMA, TOMI, Ext-WH, and DW — and provides their descriptions.

## Key facts

**akaMES Manufacturing Product Ecosystem (6 products):**

| Product | Description |
|---|---|
| **akaMES** | Intelligent MOM Solution — production management software suite; extends ERP with manufacturing capabilities |
| **akaPLM** | Product Lifecycle Management — manages and controls all product lifecycle data; tracks product development and spec changes |
| **STMA** | Asset Insight & Predictive Maintenance Software — deep insights into asset health and utilization; proactive maintenance strategies |
| **TOMI** | Accounting Software for Manufacturing — managerial accounting; quick aggregation and accurate production cost calculation |
| **Ext-WH** | Extended Warehouse Management — import/export, on-hand inventory, storage cost optimization for SMEs in manufacturing & logistics |
| **DW** | High-performance Management System with Data Warehouse — centralized data management from various enterprise sources; analytics for management decisions |
