## Summary

This is the akaVerse internal sales training deck (35 slides, August 2023), covering the full product portfolio of FPT IS's VR/AR/XR/immersive technology subsidiary. The deck presents akaVerse's company overview, 9 primary solutions, detailed solution breakdowns with competitive comparisons, case studies, and technology capabilities (Volumetric Capture, Digital Twin). All slides are bilingual (Vietnamese with English product/solution names); content is extractable.

akaVerse was founded September 2021 and operates as a dedicated XR/immersive tech unit within [[FPT IS]], primarily targeting enterprises in Vietnam and the broader Asia region.

## Key facts

**Company overview:**
- Founded: September 2021
- Team size: 50+ employees
- Scale: 82 projects, 30+ customers
- Certifications: Unity Certified Professional Artist/Programmer, Expert Technical Artist/Programmer
- Contact: Mr. Vu Duy Thanh (thanhvd8@fpt.com); FPT Tower, 10 Pham Van Bach St., Hanoi

**9 primary solutions:**
1. **Virtual Tour** — 3D immersive space tour; interior/exterior; 3DS Max + SketchUp design
2. **Virtual Shop** — 3D virtual retail environment; product showrooms; virtual try-on; linked to purchase gateway
3. **Virtual Training** — VR Training (Oculus/Pico/HTC headsets) + AR Training (mobile browser, no app)
4. **Digital Twin (Bản sao số)** — IoT-integrated digital asset management; all tangible/intangible assets on one platform
5. **Virtual Bank** — Virtual banking branch (flagship: TPBank Metabank)
6. **Virtual Event** — Online/hybrid virtual event spaces
7. **Virtual Car Showroom** — 3D automotive virtual showroom with car customization
8. **Outsourcing** — 3D/VR/AR content development services
9. **Car Customization** — Interactive 3D car configuration for automotive clients

**Additional technology capabilities (not in the 9-solution list above):**
- **QR-Web AR** — AR experiences via mobile browser; no app install; QR-triggered; Apple VisionOS compatible; hand gesture interaction
- **3D Interactive** — Product storytelling in interactive 3D; PC + mobile; works without app install; use cases: training, sales/marketing, product guides
- **Metaverse** — Persistent virtual worlds; virtual music tour, concert hall, entertainment center use cases; 3 principles: Movement, Exchange, Transform
- **Volumetric Capture** — Photogrammetry + Volumetric Video system with 60–120 DSLR cameras; creates hyper-realistic human/object captures for virtual spaces
- **Personalized Video** — AI-generated personalized video content in 50+ languages

**Virtual Tour vs. Matterport competitive comparison:**

| Feature | akaVerse Virtual Tour | Matterport |
|---|---|---|
| Input data | 3D design + optional real photos | Physical space scan only |
| Movement points | Freely renderable | Limited to actual scan points |
| Post-processing | Supported | Not supported; reflection surface issues |
| Hosting | On-prem or akaVerse server | Matterport server only |
| Domain | Customizable | Matterport domain only |
| Metrics | Preset metrics only | Fully customizable |

**Virtual Tour features:** 3D Design (3DS Max/SketchUp), Media Integration (images/video/audio/links embedded), Virtual Tour Guide (AI avatar greeter), 3rd-Party App Integration, Online Support (live video call during tour), Floor Plan navigation

**Virtual Shop features:** Virtual Showroom (3D product catalog), Virtual Shopping (360 interaction, virtual try-on), Unlimited product catalog, Technical info per product model, online purchase gateway integration

**Digital Twin industry verticals:** Commerce & Retail, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Automotive, Media & Entertainment, Education

**Two FIS business unit tracks (GS/ES):**
- Government Sector (GS): 3D design, VR, AR, 360/drone services
- Enterprise Sector (ES): Virtual Tour, 3D digitization, virtual training
