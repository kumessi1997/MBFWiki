## Summary

Meduverse is FPT IS's game-based educational solution developed in association with **Oxford University Press**. It targets primary school children (Grades 1–6), blending Oxford curriculum content with interactive games, AI-personalized learning, and competitive e-sport tournaments on a web + mobile platform.

The saleskit (EN, 21 slides) covers the product overview, 5 selling points, value proposition per stakeholder (teacher/student/parent), learning system, competition features, and three real deployment cases at FPT Schools (Đà Nẵng Aug 2024, Cầu Giấy Nov 2024, E-Sport Season 1 Feb–Apr 2025). Survey data from students, teachers, and parents is included, showing strong early adoption signals.

Meduverse is distinctly different from [[VioEdu]] (K-12 AI adaptive, broader curriculum, 20M users) — it is specifically game-first, competition-driven, Oxford-branded, and currently in early rollout at FPT Schools.

## Key facts

- **Partnership:** FPT IS + Oxford University Press (content provider)
- **Platform:** Web App + Mobile App (multi-device)
- **Target audience:** Primary school children, Grades 1–6
- **Subjects live:** English (Oxford "Family and Friends" series, Grades 1–6)
- **Subjects coming soon:** Math (Toán), Life Skills (Kỹ năng sống)
- **Contact:** support@meduverse.ai / meduverse.ai / +84-968-660-216

## 5 selling points

1. **International Standard Content** — Oxford University Press curriculum
2. **AI Technology** — Personalized learning content per individual student
3. **Evaluation System** — Progress reporting and monitoring for teachers and parents
4. **Fit Design** — Age-appropriate games; safe, healthy digital environment
5. **Appealing Approach** — Learning activities seamlessly integrated into games and competitions

## Value by stakeholder

**Teachers:** Enhanced lesson attractiveness; time/effort saving via reporting & class monitoring; automatic detailed evaluation results

**Students:** Appealing game-based lessons; proactive personalized learning at own level/pace/skill set; reward mechanisms and goal-setting boost motivation

**Parents:** Progress monitoring via reporting features; parental participation in learning; safety filtering blocks toxic internet content

## Learning system

- Content: Oxford "Family and Friends" (Grades 1–6, English); level- and skill-based exercises
- Exercise types per lesson: Vocabulary, Grammar, Songs, Pronunciation, Games (Memory Card, Jumpy, etc.)
- Lessons and interactive games aligned with learning materials to encourage passive knowledge gain and revision
- AI personalizes content to individual student level

## Competition system

## Deployment history & results

### FPT Schools Đà Nẵng — 17/08/2024
- Method: Free experience + introduction to parents
- Results: 1,000+ participants (students + parents); 350+ surveys completed; 400+ gifts given

### FPT Schools Cầu Giấy, Hà Nội — 08/11/2024 & 12/11/2024
- Method: School competition (Grade 1 & 2, all classes)
- Results: 338 students, 11 classes, 220 gifts, 338 scholarship packages given

### Meduverse E-Sport Season 1 — 02/2025 – 04/2025
- Scope: FPT Schools nationwide, Grades 1–5
- Results: **3,500 contestants · 6 schools · 50,000+ sessions**

## Survey data (FPT Schools sample)

**Students (n=330):**
- 95% like playing Meduverse (Yes: 312/330)
- Top activities liked: Learning (151) > Practice (133) > Competition (128) > Hang out with friends (96)

**Teachers (n=35):**
- 91% prefer using in teaching; 0% say No
- 100% say Meduverse content fits curriculum
- 91% prefer using for student competitions

**Parents (n=273):**
- 78% want children to use Meduverse
- 82% find competitions helpful for children
- 27% willing to purchase membership; 60% need further investigation (purchase intent low but interest high)
