## Summary

Two-document set (brochure + infographic, 3 pages total) presenting **AZINSU TPA**, FPT IS's digital platform for health insurance claims adjudication and payment. The product automates the full insurance claims lifecycle by connecting three parties: medical facilities (Cơ sở Y tế), TPA/insurance companies (Công ty bảo hiểm), and insured persons (Người được bảo hiểm).

The documents are primarily in Vietnamese with English product/technology names. All substantive content is extractable.

No named client case studies appear in either document.

## Key facts

**Product definition:**
- AZINSU TPA = Digital platform for health insurance claims adjudication & payment
- Full name of parent unit: FPT IS Digital Insurance Center (Trung tâm bảo hiểm số)
- Contact: cskh@azinsu.com · azinsu.com · Tel: 0812 002 266
- Mobile app available for insured persons (App Store + Google Play)

**7 services provided:**
1. Inpatient and outpatient hospitalization guarantee (Bảo lãnh viện phí Nội trú và Ngoại trú)
2. Health insurance claims settlement and payment
3. Multi-channel customer consultation and interaction
4. Claims data management and analysis
5. Insurance product/benefit improvement consulting
6. Investigation of suspicious fraud claims
7. Health checkup package arrangement

**4 technologies applied:**
- **RPA**: Auto-collect medical treatment data from healthcare facilities; auto-adjudicate insurance claims
- **AI/OCR**: Read medical records (invoices, cost statements, examination forms); detect fake or unsigned documents
- **Big Data**: Analyze treatment and claims data; recommend product/benefit improvements to insurers
- **Cloud**: Full cloud deployment; separate portals for insured persons, corporate buyers, hospitals, and insurers

**Competitive differentiation vs. market standard:**

| Dimension | Market standard | AZINSU |
|---|---|---|
| **Data** | Hospital manually inputs data into insurer/TPA portal; TPA re-enters into software | Auto-connects to healthcare facilities; AI/OCR reads paper claims from insured persons |
| **Claims adjudication** | Manual: check benefits, costs, limits; review treatment data; determine compensation | Automated: cross-check benefits/costs/limits; auto-detect fraud; manage & analyze data; propose compensation plan |

**Performance SLAs:**
- Average claims processing time: **5 days/case**
- On-time resolution rate: **95%**
- Hospitalization guarantee response (to healthcare facility): **15 minutes**
- Insured person claims response: **24 hours**

**Claim workflow (4-party process):**
1. Insured person visits healthcare facility
2. Healthcare facility sends guarantee request → AZINSU auto-checks benefits/limits → insurer decides on guarantee
3. Healthcare facility exports XML after treatment → AZINSU collects/reconciles data
4. Insured person submits claims request → AZINSU auto-adjudicates → insurer decides on compensation → settlement/payment → all parties notified

**FPT healthcare credentials (company-wide):**
- 20 years of healthcare IT solutions development
- 300+ healthcare facilities using FPT IS solutions
- 500+ IT specialists with healthcare experience
